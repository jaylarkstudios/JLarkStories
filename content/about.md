## Hi, I'm Lark!

I'm an artist and creator who loves bringing characters and stories to life through illustration. JLarkStories is my personal space for sharing art, talking about creative projects, and recommending the media that inspires me.

## What I Do

I primarily work in digital illustration, focusing on character art, concept design, and the occasional comic page. My tools of choice are Clip Studio Paint and Photoshop, and I work on a display tablet.

When I'm not drawing, I'm usually consuming way too many books, games, shows, and movies — and then telling everyone about them. That's what the reviews section is for.

## This Site

I built this site because I wanted a home for my work that I fully own and control. Social media is great for connecting with people, but it's not great for archiving or presenting work the way I want to. Here, I can do both.

Everything on this site is organized into three categories:

- **Art** — finished pieces, sketches, process breakdowns
- **News** — project updates, convention plans, announcements
- **Reviews** — thoughts on books, games, shows, and tools

You can also browse by **tags** to find posts about specific topics, characters, or projects.

## Get in Touch

The best way to reach me is through my social links in the footer. I'm most active on Bluesky and Telegram.

If you enjoy my work, consider checking out my [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/jlarkstories) — it's the best way to support what I do and get early access to new art.

Thanks for visiting!

*— Lark*
