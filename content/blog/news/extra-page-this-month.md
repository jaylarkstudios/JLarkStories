You'll recall that lately I've been updating the main story of *Aww, Feathers!* three times a month.  Well, every other month that we maintain our current funding level there will be four main story updates instead of three. And thanks to my awesome supporters on Patreon, this month is a bonus month! There are five Tuesdays this month again, so the fifth one will be another mini comic, but that's better than March when we had two of them in a row.

Thanks once again to everyone who has supported on Patreon! And if you haven't, please consider making just a small monthly pledge; less than a dollar even! We're just a few dollars away from our next goal of updating four times a month!

Thanks again!
