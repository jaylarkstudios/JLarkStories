As I alluded to before, *Aww, Feathers!* on Patreon has officially made the switch to Pay Up Front. This should only affect **new patrons** and **patrons increasing their pledge**. In both cases, the new pledge will take place immediately instead of waiting until the beginning of the next month.

If there are any questions or confusion over this slight change, let me know. Thanks again for your continued support!
