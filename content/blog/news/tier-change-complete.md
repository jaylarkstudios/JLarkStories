Hi there! If you're seeing this message, it means you are on one of the older, now discontinued Patreon tiers. This is just a reminder that, as detailed in [this post](https://www.patreon.com/posts/patreon-reward-22984423) I made back in December, in order to continue receiving access to exclusive bonus content, you will need to choose a new subscription level [here](http://patreon.com/join/awwfeathers). Again, Patreon doesn't allow me to move patrons to a different tier, even if it's the same price, so unless/until you switch to a new tier or cancel your pledge, you will continue to be charged the same amount each month without receiving any rewards. If you're okay with that and just want to support *Aww, Feathers!* financially, then that's great! If you want your rewards though, then remember to make the switch.

I'm sorry for any confusion or inconvenience this change may cause, but I thank you for being understanding and supportive. Ultimately this change will help me to continue to grow the comic. If there are any questions, feel free to ask in a comment or send me a private message.

Thanks again!
