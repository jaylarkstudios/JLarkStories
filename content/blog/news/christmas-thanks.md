Another year has passed us by so quickly. It was a good year for me. I've been blessed with many things this year (including a new member of the family). And of course, it's also been a great year for *Aww, Feathers!*, thanks in no small part to everyone who has supported me on Patreon this year. Thank you!

I thought I might wait a little longer before beginning my Patreon campaign, but on a whim I decided to at least set it up so that people would know it's there. I didn't expect to receive the support that I have so quickly! I would have been happy to continue posting story updates for free, albeit at a reduced frequency. But thanks to the support I've received from you, I've been able to bring us back up to a weekly update schedule! Here's hoping the trend continues. I'd love to be able to update even more frequently in the future. For now though, just know that I am very grateful!

By the way, sorry that updates have been a bit scarce on Patreon for the past little while. Be sure to check out the [guest comic event](http://www.awwfeathers.com/comics/2354381/the-babys-coming/)  happening on the website right now through the end of the year, and then we'll be back to the story in January! I've also got a few more treats planned before then, so stay tuned!

And in the meantime, have a very Merry Christmas and Happy Hollidays!

-Josh
