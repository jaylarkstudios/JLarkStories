I've been getting back into journaling lately. I've found it to be a  good practice at the end of the day to look back on the highlights, both  good and bad, as well as some things that I'm grateful for or that I  was able to accomplish. It helps me to remember the things that went  well for me, as well as the challenges I was able to overcome. And it's  just a nice way to unplug and unwind before going to bed. One thing that  has helped me stay motivated to keep up on this habit has been to add a  little bit of fun to my daily journal entry by drawing a quick image that represents something that happened that day in the form of a single  panel comic.

I've been doing this for a little over a month now,  and it's been fun for me. I thought it might also be fun to share with my patrons. I won't be sharing the journal entries themselves, cuz  that's none y'all's beeswax! But I have been posting the little comics that I've been including with each entry. I spend no more than 2-3 minutes on each one. They are all very off the  cuff, unplanned, and unpolished, which is why I am informally referring  to these as [Scribble Comics](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=ScribbleComic). I make no guarantees about the quality of the artwork or the writing. But they just might make you smile.

The  first batch of scribble comics are available now to patrons at the  [Awwthusiast](https://join.awwfeathers.com/awwthusiast) level and above, and I will aim to post future ones to  Patreon the morning after they are drawn. These will be in addition to regular comic updates, which will continue to be released to the public.

Hope you enjoy!
