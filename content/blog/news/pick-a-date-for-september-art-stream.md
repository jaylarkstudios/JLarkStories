It’s time to set a date and time for the September art stream! This stream will be open to all patrons at the Awwrt Lover level and above. The theme for September will be VILLAINS! Now, I know what you're thinking: sounds like kind of a spooky theme. Wouldn't that fit October better? Maybe, but I'll be participating in another, non-villainy related art challenge during the month of October. So for September, tell me which character from *Aww, Feathers!* you think would fit as your favorite villain. Could be a villain from a Disney film, comic book, video game, or any other medium. Feel free to comment or send me a message with your suggestions for which characters and scenarios to draw for this theme. Or if you plan on attending live, pick which date you can attend and bring your ideas to the stream!

Below are the available dates and times. You can pick more than one, so please select all those that would work for you. The poll will remain open for one week, after which the art stream will be scheduled to accommodate as many patrons as possible. The stream will be recorded for those unable to attend (or who would like to rewatch it).

Note: while the options provided reflect the dates and times that I tend to be available, please let me know if none of them work for you, but you would still like to watch the stream live. While it may not be possible to set a time that works for everybody every month, I will work with you to find a date and time that will work for you in a future month.
