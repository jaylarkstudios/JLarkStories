Just a reminder if you haven't checked it out yet, that we have an official Discord server now! Wanted to give patrons an opportunity to check it out before opening it up to the general public. Just click the link below and you'll be admitted!

[https://discord.gg/CpUAB7U](https://discord.gg/CpUAB7U)

Oh! And one other thing. If connect your Discord account with Patreon, you'll automatically be given the "Patron" role in the server. Currently all it does is colors your username in the server, but it's possible a patron-only channel or two may be opened up in the future, so be sure to connect your accounts! You can do so by going to Account Settings > Account.
