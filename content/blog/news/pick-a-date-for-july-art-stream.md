It’s time to set a date and time for the July art stream! This stream will be open to all patrons at the Awwrt Lover level and above. The theme for July will be FREEDOM!! I've been wanting to do some patriotic themed drawings for a while now, and July is an appropriate time for it here in the United States! Still, I'm also open to other interpretations of the theme. Feel free to comment or send me a message with your suggestions for which characters and scenarios to draw for this theme. Or if you plan on attending live, pick which date you can attend and bring your ideas to the stream!

Below are the available dates and times. You can pick more than one, so please select all those that would work for you. The art stream will be scheduled to accommodate as many patrons as possible. The stream will be recorded for those unable to attend (or who would like to rewatch it).

Note: while the options provided reflect the dates and times that I tend to be available, please let me know if none of them work for you, but you would still like to watch the stream live. While it may not be possible to set a time that works for everybody every month, I will work with you to find a date and time that will work for you in a future month.
