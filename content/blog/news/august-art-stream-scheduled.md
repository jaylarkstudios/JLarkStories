The date for the August art stream for [Awwrt Lovers](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) has been set for **Tuesday, August 11 at ****[7pm MT](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/usa/utah)**. A link will be posted the day of the stream. The theme will be "Words of Hope". Is there a quote or passage of text that inspires you or brings you comfort? How would you illustrate those words? Leave your suggestions below, or bring them to the stream! The video will be available for those who'd like to watch it after the stream ends, and the resulting images from the stream will be posted on Patreon throughout the month.

If you would like to participate, be sure to join the [Awwrt Lovers](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) tier and mark your calendar! You'll also gain access to all previous drawings in the *Inked Feathers* collection, as well as behind-the-scenes and other bonus content for the comic!

See you then!
