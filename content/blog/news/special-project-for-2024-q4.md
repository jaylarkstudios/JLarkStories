Happy October! The leaves are turning, the bones are rattling, and artists all over the world are prepping their pens! As for me, I've got my own special project planned for the coming months!

First of all, while I did find I could keep up with the release schedule of two updates per month for *Aww, Feathers!* that I had for the first half of 2024, I also found that it left me with very little time for anything else. I love *Aww, Feathers!* and have no plans to stop it anytime soon. But I don't want it to be the only thing I do. So I've made the decision to pull it back to once a month again for the time being. If I find that I've built up enough of a buffer in the future, I may occasionally release an extra page here and there. But with the extra time that I have, I'll be able to work on other things like improving my website, taking more commissions, and telling more stories!

For a while now, I've wanted to try my hand at writing a short story in graphic novel format. *Aww, Feathers!* is a long-form story, and there are many more chapters planned. But I would also like to try my hand at writing other, shorter stories. So in lieu of participating in Inktober or NaNoWriMo, I've made a plan to write a short, simple story, which will be the first one of its kind that I've done to completion, start to finish! I've already got a basic outline of the story planned. I haven't completely settled on a title yet, but for now I'll be referring to it by the working title, ***Mag and Fang***. I won't be spoiling any of the story just yet, especially since it's not fully written yet! But I can tell you it will have animal characters, a more simplified art style, and a light-hearted plot that's especially kid-friendly, but intended to be enjoyed by all ages!

My plan for the month of October is to write the script for *Mag and Fang* as well as come up with designs for the main characters and establish the look and feel for the story. I'm aiming for a story that is around 12-16 pages long. Then for November, I plan to complete the first rough draft of the short graphic novel, including sketches. By then I also hope to determine what level of color and detail to complete it in, because during the month of December, I plan to revise the draft as needed, polish it up, and prepare it for publishing. If all goes well (fingers crossed!) I hope to have a new short story to share with you all by the beginning of 2025!

While the majority of my time will be taken up by this *Mag and Fang* during the rest of this year, I'll still set aside at least a little bit of time each day to make progress on the next page of *Aww, Feathers!* Even so, with this new project and all the holiday plans for the rest of the year, I may end up taking a break from updating the story during November and December. I also won't be opening up any art commissions until after *Mag and Fang* is complete. To all my Awwthusiasts out there, I appreciate your patience. But don't worry, you'll still be hearing from me!

If you'd like to see my progress on this brand new story before it's released publicly, you've got a couple options! First, catch my art streams, which are typically  Tuesday and Friday mornings, at [https://twitch.tv/jlarkstories](https://twitch.tv/jlarkstories) so you can watch the story take shape in real time! Secondly, you can visit [https://join.awwfeathers.com](https://join.awwfeathers.com) to join my Patreon at the Awwthusiast level or above to see character sketches, rough drafts, and more bonus material, in addition to the hundreds of bonus posts for *Aww, Feathers!* that already exists for patrons!

Whether you watch me on twitch, subscribe on Patreon, or simply continue to follow my art and share it with others, thank you so much for the privilege of sharing my stories with you! And with that kickoff, have a very happy fall, y'all!! 🎃🍂☕
