Hey! I realize this is short notice, but it’s time to set a date and time for the March art stream! This stream will be open to all patrons at the Awwrt Lover level and above. The theme for March will be Zootopia! You may recall that I recently did a stream in which I ranked my [top ten favorite animated films](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5gZB6xiJsrk). Spoiler alert: Zootopia made the number one position on that list! So I'm going to do some *Aww, Feathers! *themed art during the stream that draws on Zootopia for inspiration. Now, you might be thinking, how's that going to work? *Aww, Feathers! *already has intelligent, clothes-wearing (well, mostly), anthropomorphic animal characters. However, one thing that Zootopia does very well that I think I could learn from is *world building!* Everywhere you look in that movie you can see how the world has been meticulously crafted to meet the diverse yet specific needs of its many different inhabitants. So I want to look at some of those features and think about how I could apply those lessons to the world my own characters live in!

Feel free to comment or send me a message with your suggestions for which characters and scenarios to draw for this theme. Or if you plan on attending live, pick which date you can attend and bring your ideas to the stream!

Below are the available dates and times. You can pick more than one, so please select all those that would work for you. The poll will remain open for one week, after which the art stream will be scheduled to accommodate as many patrons as possible. The stream will be recorded for those unable to attend (or who would like to rewatch it).

Note: while the options provided reflect the dates and times that I tend to be available, please let me know if none of them work for you, but you would still like to watch the stream live. While it may not be possible to set a time that works for everybody every month, I will work with you to find a date and time that will work for you in a future month.
