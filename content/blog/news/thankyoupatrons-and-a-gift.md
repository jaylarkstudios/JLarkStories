This year marked the **fifth year** that *Aww, Feathers!* has been running! I want to thank all of you for your support, and especially my patrons. Subscribing on Patreon at any level demonstrates to me that you care deeply about the comic and other projects I'm working on. Thank you so much! It means a lot to me that I've been able to produce something that has brightened days and touched lives.

To show my appreciation to my patrons I've prepared a simple gift. Please check your messages on Patreon where you will find a dropbox link to a downloadable archive of high-quality scans of this year's Inktober drawings for your viewing pleasure. Anyone who subscribes in November will also get this gift, so if you've been on the fence, now's a great time to try!

Thank you all once again! I look forward to many more years to come!
