Very important update for anyone who is currently an *Aww, Feathers! *supporter, or who may have been interested in becoming one: starting in April 2025, I will no longer be posting Patreon-exclusive content. Instead, all "bonus content" will be made public. I will be changing all Patreon tiers to be "support only", meaning Patreon supporters will no longer receive anything other than the opportunity to financially support my art and comics. Those who wish to discontinue their Patreon membership may do so at any time, and absolutely no hard feelings at all; it means so much to me that you supported me for any length of time! But anyone who wishes to continue their Patreon membership even without any bonus content may also do so, and I super appreciate any who do!

**Why have I made this decision?**

Don't worry, I will still be working on art and comics, including *Aww, Feathers!* But I've decided that I need to focus more on my writing, art, and audience building before I spend more effort on monetizing bonus content. I still plan to occasionally release things like behind the scenes posts, timelapse videos, and bonus drawings, but they will now be available to the public instead of being behind a paywall. I believe this is a step I need to take in order to focus more of my time and energy on improving my craft.

**What will happen to my existing content on Patreon?**

Starting in April, all existing Patreon posts that were restricted only to patrons will be made public. In addition, any future bonus content will be public as well.

**What do you need to do?**

If you want to remain a patron at your current level in order to continue to support my art and comics, you don't need to do anything. You will continue to pay the same amount monthly.

If you want to remain a patron but would like to change your tier or edit your pledge amount, go to Settings, then Memberships, then click View Details next to my page. From there you can click "Switch to another tier", or click Edit next to "Payment amount".

If you want to cancel your membership, follow the steps above, but click "Cancel membership" instead.

**How else can you support me?**

One of the best ways you can support my projects is by sharing them with others. A sincere recommendation from a friend can make a huge difference towards them becoming a future reader and fan! You can also follow and interact with me on my socials. At the moment I'm most active on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/jlarkstories.com) and [Telegram](https://t.me/awwfeathers). For a complete list of places you can find me, check out [jlarkstories.com](https://jlarkstories.com).

If you're looking for other ways to support me financially, consider getting a book or pin from my [store](http://store.awwfeathers.com). (I plan to eventually add a few other things like ebooks and stickers, so keep an eye on my socials for when those come out!) Or if you enjoy watching live streams, I typically stream 2-3 times per week over on [twitch](http://twitch.tv/jlarkstories), and you can support me there by subscribing or cheering with bits. Lastly, if you're interested in a custom art piece, you can commission me. Reach out to me on any of my socials for commission details.

**Will I ever post patron-exclusive content in the future?**

Perhaps someday. But it may be a while. I'm going to wait until doing so would produce a more significant and sustainable amount of income, so that I can actually dedicate more time towards creating exclusive content for patrons without taking time away from my comics.

Until such a time, I am at peace with this decision. I honestly think it is the right thing to do, and I look forward to honing my craft.

Thanks for reading! I will post a few reminders over the coming months to make sure everyone is aware of the change. Take care!
