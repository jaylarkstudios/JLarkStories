Greetings! I hope you had a wonderful Thanksgiving break and are gearing up for the holiday season! In an effort to be as transparent as possible to you, who are among the most devoted readers of *Aww, Feathers!,* I wanted to give you plenty of advance notice to be aware of the following changes to Patreon reward tiers that will be taking place over the next few months.

In short, in 2019 the monthly cost of reward tiers will be going up ***for new patrons only*** through the month of March. Then, beginning in April, they will go up for ***all patrons***. For more details, read on.

**Price Changes**

- Bonus Content: $1 ➡ $2
- Sketchbook Club Bronze: $3 ➡ $5
- Sketchbook Club Silver: $5 ➡ $10
- Sketchbook Club Gold: $10 ➡ $15  
- Cameo Appearance: $15 ➡ $25 

Starting January 1, 2019, the current Patreon tiers will be locked and new tiers will be introduced. **What does this mean for current patrons?** For now, things will remain more or less the same. Current patrons will continue to receive access to the same rewards they were receiving before for the same monthly amount they were pledging before. **What does this mean for new patrons?** Those making a new pledge or those changing their pledge will only have access to the new reward tiers. So if you've been considering trying out a higher tier than the one you're currently at, update your pledge before the end of the year to lock in the current, lower price for the next few months. After January 1st, if you change or remove your pledge, you will no longer have access to the old reward tiers.

Starting March 31, 2019, the current Patreon tiers will be removed entirely. **What does this mean for current patrons?** Patreon does not allow creators to move patrons from an old reward tier to a new one, even if it's the same price. So anyone still on an old reward tier when this change occurs will be moved to the "no benefit" section, meaning they will continue to be charged, but will no longer receive any reward. To prevent this from happening, be sure to edit your pledge before then. You can see how to do so [here](https://support.patreon.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000126286).

I will give several reminders leading up to both dates mentioned above to those patrons still on the old reward tiers.

**Why the change?** There are two primary reasons. One is the fact that processing fees eat up a large percentage of smaller pledges; sometimes *more than half* of a $1 pledge! But the larger a pledge amount is, the smaller the percentage is that gets taken out. The second reason is simply a matter of sustainability. I need to make sure that each piece of content (including bonus content) is worth the time I spend creating it, especially as long as it isn't my primary source of income.

**What if I can't afford the new price for my current tier?** I completely understand if that's the case. Feel free to stay on your current tier through the month of March. Then, if you need to, you can certainly change your tier to one of the newer tiers you can afford, or even take a break for a while. I hope you'll continue to read *Aww, Feathers!* and consider renewing your Patreon pledge when able to do so.

**What can I do if I can't afford $2 per month, but still want to support *****Aww, Feathers*****?** First of all, that's great! Thank you for your willingness to support the comic in whatever way you can! If you will be unable to continue your monthly pledge after this change but would still like to support *Aww, Feathers!* financially, you could consider remaining a patron for just a month or two at a time, then take some time away to save up, and then rejoin again. If that's too much of a hassle, you could leave a periodic donation on my [ko-fi](http://ko-fi.com/awwfeathers). Or you could save up your money to buy a [commission](http://www.awwfeathers.com/commish/) or [merch item](https://gumroad.com/inejwstine) from me. Finally, [contact Patreon](https://twitter.com/patreon) directly and let them know that you would like more options for those who can only afford to pledge a small amount each month.

Thanks again for your understanding and support! I can't emphasize enough that support from patrons helps make continued updates to *Aww, Feathers!* possible. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to me, either in a comment or message.

Cheers!
-Lark
