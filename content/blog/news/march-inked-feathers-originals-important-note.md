Silver Level patrons may look through the [available Inked Feathers originals](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=Available) and choose one for themselves! Please make your selections by April 10, or just wait and you'll receive a surprise!

Please note that during the COVID-19 pandemic we'll be doing things a bit differently. It's not 100% clear how well the virus can be transmitted via surfaces through the mail system. In addition, to my knowledge neither I nor anyone in my family have been infected. However, **out of an abundance of caution**, I will not be sending physical drawings in the mail until after the pandemic has started to decline, **unless you specifically ask me to**. If you would like me to send your Inked Feathers original in the mail as normal, please message me to let me know. Otherwise, I will simply hold on to your originals each month and then send them all together at the appropriate time.

Thanks for doing all you can to keep yourself and those around you safe and healthy!
