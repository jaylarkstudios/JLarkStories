Happy February all! I have a few updates for you:

- 
- This morning I sent out the February postcards, so *Feather Mail* patrons should receive them within the next few days.
- 
- We picked up a couple new patrons for this month! Welcome, and thank you so much for your support!
- 
- Because of the new contributions, we reached our first goal, which was to remove 3rd party ads from the site, so enjoy! They will stay down for the entire month of February, as well as any month we continue to hit the same goal.
- 
- I made a couple changes last month to the goals and rewards. I changed our next milestone to having an extra update every other month instead of a new wallpaper every other month. I think that's what readers would prefer anyway. (If anyone is really torn up about not getting *Aww, Feathers!* themed wallpapers, I apologize.  Maybe that can be a future milestone.)
- I also added the fifty cents patron level for anyone on a really tight budget, but would still like to support the comic in some way. Again, it may not seem like a lot, but it adds up quickly.

That's all for now. Thanks again to all of you! Have a great month!
