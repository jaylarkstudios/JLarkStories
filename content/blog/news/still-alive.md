Greetings! I'm coming to you on a Friday for a long overdue update! I had mentioned previously that I would be moving with my family soon. Well, that has happened! We are now living in our new house. We are very happy to be here, but it has also been a very difficult experience. I never knew moving could be so hard on both a physical and emotional level. But we've also had a lot of support from friends and family. I also took a break from art and comics for a while, and honestly I'm glad that I did. Not only was a lot of my equipment packed away, but being able to focus as much as possible on the move was very helpful. We still have a lot of unpacking to do, but I'm finally ready to start easing my way back into my comics.

So what's next? Well, I will be attending Anthro Weekend Utah once again! I will continue taking a break from *Aww, Feathers!* updates until after AWU, so you can expect the next page on August 1st, but before then I'll post some bonus content for the most recent page. You can also find me online at jlarkstories.com for updates from the con and whatever else I've been up to lately.

Until next time!
