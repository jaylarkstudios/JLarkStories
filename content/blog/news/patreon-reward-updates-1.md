There have been a few changes to the Patreon rewards. Based on the reward levels of current patrons, these changes will not negatively impact anybody who is currently pledging their support. However, a few patrons will be entitled to an extra reward. Here are the changes:

- The fifty cent reward level has been removed. This was a decision made by the patreon staff, because after processing fees there would be almost nothing left of pledges under a dollar. We haven't had any patrons at this level, so the reward of being added to the site's "Thank You" page has been rolled into the $1 level.
- The $6 level (International Feather Mail) has also been removed, and the $5 level will now include international shipping. Now no matter where you are in the world, if you would like to receive a postcard, you'd choose the same "Feather Mail" option.
- A new reward level has been added. Every patron who pledges $3 or more is now eligible for the "Personalized Message" reward. A small banner has been added to few places around the website (including on the welcome page and underneath each comic page). Instead of 3rd party ads, these will be used for announcements and reminders related to *Aww, Feathers!* However, there's also room some customized messages from patrons! Want to leave a friendly greeting? Congratulate someone for an anniversary, graduation, or other accomplishment? Or just show off your support for the comic? At this level, your customized message will be in rotation with the other announcement banners for one month, or if desired may be displayed with greater frequency for a shorter time period.(Messages are subject to approval and may not be used for advertising or promotional purposes.) For more info about this new site feature, check out the recent news post at [http://www.awwfeathers.com/news-archive/posts/143526/new-site-feature-announcement-bar/](http://www.awwfeathers.com/news-archive/posts/143526/new-site-feature-announcement-bar/)

If there are any questions or concerns about these changes, let me know! Thanks again!

-Josh
