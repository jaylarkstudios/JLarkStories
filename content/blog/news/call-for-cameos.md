**Edit:** All slots are now full. Thank you so much! To those who are interested in a future cameo appearance, keep an eye out! Some may be only available to patrons at a certain level or higher, while others may be available to patrons at any level (like this one). But they will *always* be made available to patrons first!

Greetings! This is a quick message to let you all know that there's an opportunity to appear in an upcoming page of *Aww, Feathers!* If selected, your character would appear in the background of a future comic page. Here are some examples from previous pages:

- [Example 1](https://awwfeathers.com/comics/page165/)
- [Example 2](https://awwfeathers.com/comics/page201/)
- [Example 3](https://awwfeathers.com/comics/page210/)
- [Example 4](https://awwfeathers.com/comics/page241/)

For this scene, cameos will simply appear in the background of school campus, in and/or outside school buildings, doing school-related things. I have room for at least three cameos, maybe more. If you're interested, all you need to do is

- Be a current patron **at any level!** If you are not currently a patron but are interested in a cameo appearance, you may join now to be considered, and also gain access to all the other perks available to patrons! (If you are not a patron but have an unused cameo reward from previous years, you may also join in.)
- Comment on this post with a reference to the character you'd like to appear.

Please note that in order to make a cameo appearance, your character must fit within the established rules of the Awwfeatherverse. Specifically that means

- No more than four limbs and one tail. A flying species can have arms or wings, but they can't have arms *and* wings as separate limbs.
- No characters/species belonging to other properties (like Pokemon, Digimon, etc.) or created and widely used by the furry fandom at large (like sergals, protogens, synths, etc.).
- No bugs, fish, or prehistoric species. Although if your character is based on a prehistoric species that could resemble a modern day species (for example, a raptor could resemble a lizard), then it may still be eligible. Feel free to ask.
- Characters with prosthetics are allowed provided they are realistic for the modern context in which they appear.
- Both bipedal and quadrupedal characters are allowed. Bipedal characters must be wearing modern looking clothes.
- Dragons and hybrid species (such as pegasai, griffins, jakalopes, etc.) are allowed as long as they follow the other guidelines above.
- You may modify the design of your character in order to fit these guidelines. I have final say on what characters would be a good fit for the scene I'm making.

For this appearance, priority will be given to those who have not yet had a cameo appearance in the past. But if you have, still feel free to make a request, and if there are still spots available, you may be considered! If there are more patrons who want a cameo than spots available, they will be given in order of patron level.

**Please note!** By accepting a cameo appearance, you grant me permission to include your character in the comic page and to display it anywhere that page appears, including on the website, on social media, and in print. You will, of course, keep the rights to your own character. At my discretion I may provide you with some options for what your character may be doing in the scene, but you agree to give me the final decision on how they will appear.

And that's all! These spots will be open until April 21 at the latest, so let me know soon if you'd like to appear. Thanks!
