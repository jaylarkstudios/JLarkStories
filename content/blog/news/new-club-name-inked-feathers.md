Those patrons who contribute a little extra each month gain access to bonus *Aww, Feathers!* themed artwork. Unlike the entries from my sketchbook I share periodically throughout the month, these are planned and refined pen and ink drawings. Previously known as the "Aww, Feathers Sketchbook Club," I'm officially re-branding it to "Inked Feathers!" So be on the lookout for new entries soon!

If you're not currently a member of the *Inked Feathers* club, [check out a preview](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?tag=SketchbookClubPreview) of some of last year's entries. Then [join today](https://www.patreon.com/join/awwfeathers) for access to the dozens of exclusive past drawings, as well as new ones every month! Become a Silver or Gold member to receive one of the original drawings in the mail each month, or even make requests for future drawings!
