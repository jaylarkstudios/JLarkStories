Greetings to all my wonderful readers, followers, and supporters! I have important news to share about what I have planned for *Aww, Feathers! *and other projects of mine for the coming year.

First, let me talk about my short story project from the end of 2024, *Mag & Fang*. I think it went very well! I was able to finish a first draft of the entire story. I still need to finish uploading draft pages for patrons to view. After that... the story is going to sit for a while. They say time is the best editor, so I'm going to come back to it after a while to give it a second look, and determine what can be improved, while also gathering feedback from my writing group. As to what form the final story will take, I haven't yet decided. But I'm leaning towards a simpler, looser art style than I've typically used. But that final decision won't come until after the writing has been polished.

Second, I have a very important update regarding *Aww, Feathers!* Speaking of time being the best editor, enough time has passed since I wrote the current chapter I've been working on that, along with some very helpful feedback I've gotten, I've decided the chapter needs some reworking. The bad news is, that means the comic will need to continue to be on hold for a while. The good news is, it will be even better as a result once the time comes to continue working on it. I love *Aww, Feathers!* I often get excited (some might say "easily distracted") by various projects. But *Aww, Feathers!* is the one that keeps calling me back. It's important to me. So important, that I'm willing to put updates on hold in order to make it as good as it can be. At this time I don't know when the story will resume. In fact, I don't even know how much of the current chapter I'll be able to reuse; I may even start fresh if that's what it takes. But believe me when I say that I feel like this is the right move. In fact, I feel like this is an opportunity to level up my writing skills, and I'm excited to take that journey!

While I work on rewriting the current chapter though, I'll also be working on reformatting older pages. This is both to make them easier to read on mobile devices, and to prepare them for new ebook releases! More news about that will be coming in the near future! In the meantime, you can see these reformatted pages on my socials (most reliably on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/jlarkstories.com) and [Telegram](https://t.me/awwfeathers)), and [Awwthusiast](http://join.awwfeathers.com) supporters will continue to receive behind the scenes material for each page as thanks for your continued support. It really means a lot!

Lastly, I do plan to take a few art commissions throughout the year, but probably scaled back to some degree in order to allow me ample time to work on my writing. Again, follow my socials for news about commissions, and to see new artwork from me when it's completed.

Thanks for taking the time to read. I wish you all the best on your endeavors for the coming year as I continue to work on my own!

-Lark
