Hey all,

Patrons affected by this new policy should be receiving an email from Patreon in the near future, but I wanted to let you know well in advance. **Starting July 1**, Patreon will be required to collect sales tax from some patrons, depending both on where the patron lives and what rewards they receive. From what I can see, this will most likely **not effect** the majority of *Aww, Feathers!* patrons, particularly those at the $5 level and below. Because those at the $10 level and above receive physical rewards, those at that level may see an additional sales tax added to their monthly pledge. Because the amount of sales tax varies from country to country and even from state to state (sometimes even zip code to zip code; taxes, am I right?!), it's impossible to say exactly how much it will be. However, Patreon is rolling out some new tools for creators to specify exactly what is included in each tier, in order to optimize the exact amount that is taxable. For example, (again depending on your location) $10 patrons may not be taxed on the entire amount since not all the rewards included are physical. I'll make any needed adjustments as soon as these tools are available in order to save you as much money as possible and then post an update.

If you have any questions, please see [this article](https://support.patreon.com/hc/en-us/articles/360043054911) from Patreon that goes into much more detail. If you have any other questions, please feel free to leave a comment or send me a message. (The same goes for questions of any other kind, as always.)

By the way, expect more news about the *Aww, Feathers!* and Patreon rewards real soon! Sorry if things have seemed a bit quiet lately; it's been a tricky couple months as you can probably imagine! But I'm hanging in there and still hard at work to provide you with the entertainment you've come to expect!

Thanks again for all your support!
-Lark
