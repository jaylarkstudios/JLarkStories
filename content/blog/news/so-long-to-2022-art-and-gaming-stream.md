Got any plans for New Years Eve? Stop by my "So Long to 2022 Stream" any tie between [9pm MT and midnight](https://www.timeanddate.com/time/zones/mst) tomorrow! I'll be doing some art and playing a game or two!

I'm gonna keep things flexible, but currently my plan is as follows:

- 9pm - Patron sketch stream
- 10pm - Art commission stream
- 11pm - Game stream (Spiritfarer)

Patrons at the [Awwrt Lover](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) level or higher may make sketch requests! Simply tell me a character you'd like to have drawn, and how they would celebrate the new year! (One character per sketch.) Priority will be given to those present for the stream, but if you're unable to make it, leave a comment with your sketch request, and if there's no one ahead of you, I'll do yours!

If I don't receive any patron sketch requests, I'll swap the 9pm and 10pm hours to give folks a bit more time to show up. For the art commission stream I'll be working on a musclefur commission I received (SFW as always). What better way to get pumped for your health and fitness goals for next year! 💪😁

Finally, at 11pm I plan to start playing the game *Spiritfarer*, which I've been intrigued with since I first heard about it. It looks lovely and wholesome and of course fun. Just the thing to ring in the new year! And of course, when the time gets close, we can all do the countdown together! 😄🥳

So join me tomorrow night starting at 9pm MT at [twitch.tv/jlarkstories](https://twitch.tv/jlarkstories) to join in the fun with me! Also, be sure to follow me on Twitch so you get notified of this and future streams!

See you tomorrow!
