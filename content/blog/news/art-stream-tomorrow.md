Howdy folks! It's been a minute, hasn't it! But I'm ready to get back into streaming, and excited to launch our first public *Inked Feathers* stream!

**Schedule:** [Tuesday, Nov 22, 7:30pm MT](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/usa/utah) (Tomorrow!)
**Duration:** about 1 hour
**Theme:** Pokemon!
**Link:** [twitch.tv/jlarkstories](https://www.twitch.tv/jlarkstories) 

Pokemon Scarlet and Violet have come out! Sadly, I won't be playing it due to time constraints. But, to celebrate a new group of Pokemon trainers embarking on yet another adventure (and commiserate the fact that I won't be joining them) I'll be working on some Pokemon themed drawings for the *Inked Feathers* collection!

This stream is open to all! If you are at the [Awwrt Lover](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) tier or above, you may make a request! It may include your own character. It just needs to fit with the theme above. So come prepared with your request! Or if you won't be able to make it to the stream, leave a comment below for what you'd like drawn. Please note that I may not have time to draw everyone's request, depending on how many there are. Priority will be given as follows:

- First, patrons who attend the stream live, unless they received a drawing during the previous stream.
- Next, patrons not present for the stream, but who commented with a request.
- Finally, any remaining slots will be up for grabs to patrons or higher who already received a drawing during the previous stream.

See you then!
