Greetings folks! Man, what happened to the time? The third quarter of 2022 is already nearly done. But I wanted to let you all know what the plans are for whatever's left of it!.

**The Next Chapter of *****Aww, Feathers!***

Last quarter I finished polishing the script for the next chapter of *Aww, Feathers!* It took some extra time, but I can tell you it's much better now for it! I'm really excited to work on this one, as there are some really great moments of character development that I think you'll appreciate! So the question remains: when will the next chapter be done? The bad news is, much as I'd like to say otherwise, it will not be done by the end of this quarter. However, I have updated my comic process, including some things that could make things go more quickly. So my plan is to finish the first page completely and measure how long it takes to complete it. Normally I do the penciling for all of a chapter's pages first, then inks, then colors, etc. That part of the process I plan on keeping the same; it helps me catch anything that might need to be adjusted earlier in the process. But finishing the first page all the way through will give me an idea of how long I can expect the rest of the chapter to take. I'll keep you posted as always!

**Writing Future Chapters**

Looking ahead, the chapter that comes after the next is another big one. I've already finished mapping it out, so while I work on the artwork for the current chapter, I'll also be writing the script for the next one. I also want to have the next three chapters at least planned so I know what's on the horizon. Focusing on the planning and writing stages this far in advance should help make sure everything's good and polished by the time I'm ready for artwork for those ones as well.

**More *****Lark's Nest***** Comics**

The recent "throwback" chapter just wrapped up. I plan to take a couple weeks off from posting comics in order to work on the next chapter. But after that break, I plan to make a few more comics featuring members of my family, because they're quicker to do and also fun! I've tentatively dubbed these mini comics with the title *The Lark's Nest* (though that could change in the future). They might have their own website someday; for now they'll continue to reside in the bonus section of the *Aww, Feathers!* website.

**Commissions**

I'll be continuing to offer art commissions in order to help pay for a comic creators meetup I'll be attending later this year that I'm really excited for! I'll give more details about that as it gets closer. For now, feel free to leave a comment or direct message if you'd like to inquire about a commission, and I'll let you know what's available!

**Packing...**

On the home front, things have been busy as ever. This is in part because we are preparing to move sometime next year! It may seem a ways off, but we have a lot of cleaning, de-junking, and eventually packing to do. I only mention it here because that's been taking a sizable chunk of my time lately, and it will continue to do so for a while yet. So as always, I appreciate your continued patience!

That's all for now. I look forward to diving into the rest of this quarter and keeping you all up to date on how things are coming!
