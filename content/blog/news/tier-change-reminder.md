Hi there! If you're seeing this message, it means you are on one of the older, currently locked Patreon tiers. This is just a reminder that, as detailed in [this post](https://www.patreon.com/posts/patreon-reward-22984423) I made back in December, at the end of this month, these older tiers will be going away. In order to continue receiving access to exclusive bonus content, you will need to choose a new subscription level [here](http://patreon.com/join/awwfeathers). Again, Patreon doesn't allow me to move patrons to a different tier, even if it's the same price, so unless/until you switch to a new tier or cancel your pledge, you will continue to be charged the same amount each month without receiving access. If you're okay with that and just want to support *Aww, Feathers!* financially, then that's great! If you want your rewards though, then remember to make the switch by the beginning of April.

I'm sorry for any confusion or inconvenience this change may cause, but I thank you for being understanding and supportive. Ultimately this change will help me to continue to grow the comic. I'll post one more reminder as we get closer to the end of the month. If there are any questions, feel free to ask in a comment or send me a private message.

Thanks again!
