Gold Inked Feathers members, it's time to make your suggestions or requests for the month of August. The theme for next month is simple: staying cool! How do the cast members of *Aww, Feathers!* stay cool during the hot summer months? Respond with either a comment or a message with which character you'd like to see and what they're doing to beat the heat! Your idea will become one of next month's Inked Feathers drawings and the original will be sent to you at the end of the month. If you decide you'd like a different drawing however, you have the option to switch.
