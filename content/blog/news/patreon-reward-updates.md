Greetings Awwthusiasts!

This is a quick update to let you know that there are a few changes in the works with regards to the rewards you receive as patrons. Read on for more info!

First, all **Awwthusiasts** and above will periodically have the ability to vote on certain background elements in upcoming comic pages. For example, say there's a scene with a poster on the wall of an in-world music band. Patrons could make suggestions and vote on the band's name, the species and appearance of the band members, what instruments they play, etc. If the characters are going to a restaurant, you might get to vote on what kind of food is served. Things like that. Just a fun way to have a bit of influence on the outcome of certain pages!

Second, I will continue holding monthly art streams to add to the growing collection of *Inked Feathers* drawings. However, they will now be open to the public instead of being exclusive to those at the **Awwrt Lover** tier. Instead, Awwrt Lovers will now have the ability to request that their own characters be featured in the drawings, instead of being limited to only requesting characters from *Aww, Feathers!* The only caveats are (1) the drawing still needs to fit with the monthly theme, (2) if multiple patrons have a drawing request, priority will be given to those who've gone the longest without receiving one, and (3) while it's possible to get a drawing request multiple months in a row, each one should feature a different character in order to keep a good variety. Also, if no patron claims a drawing for a given month, it may be made available to a non-patron for the regular cost of an art commission. Note that due to the time-sensitive nature of *Inked Feathers* art streams, "unclaimed requests" don't roll over from month to month. However, you will still have access to the entire collection of previous drawings, which will remain exclusive to the Awwrt Lover tier and above as before.

**Awwrt Collectors** will continue to accumulate art credits that can be redeemed for art commissions. However, I've simplified the way this process works. Instead of having a separate price for both USD and art credits, art credits are now simply worth a straight $20 each. That makes this tier the perfect way to gain access to all the bonus content available to Awwrt Lovers, while also saving up for an art commission. And the best part is, you'll pay less than you would have by doing both separately!

As always, if you have any questions about these changes, or about your patron benefits in general, feel free to reach out any time. Thanks for reading!

-Lark
