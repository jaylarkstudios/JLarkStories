Hey folks, just a heads up in case you haven't heard. Starting in November 2024, Apple is requiring Patreon to pay a 30% fee for any subscriptions made while using an iOS device. Neither I nor Patreon wants this. Fortunately, there are some simple ways around the price increase.

**This will not affect current subscriptions!** If you make no change to your current membership, you will not be charged any extra.

**This does not affect subscriptions made via a desktop browser, or the Patreon Android app!** If you subscribe or change your subscription on any device *other than* an iOS device, you will not be charged any extra.

Patreon has given creators the option to add the 30% increase to our tier prices *only for those subscribing on the iOS app*, or to just take the 30% out of our profits. Since it's easy enough for subscribers to get around the increase, I've opted for the former option. The short of it is, if you are interested in subscribing or changing your current subscription, **do so in a desktop web browser or on an Android device to avoid paying the additional fee!**
