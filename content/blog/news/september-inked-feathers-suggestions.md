The last of the summer months is nearly behind us. With that comes a brief but precious period during which the days start to get cooler but we're not yet in the throws of the coming holiday season. What activities do you participate in during this time? How do you think the cast of *Aww, Feathers!* passes the month of September? (When they're not busy with classes, that is!)

I'll be doing something a little bit different for September. I'll be drawing on some mid-tone colored paper and using a white pen for highlights in addition to my black pens for drawing. If you have any suggestions for what I should draw next month, let me know in a message or comment!
