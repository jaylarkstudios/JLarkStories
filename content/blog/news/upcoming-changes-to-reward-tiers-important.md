Hey folks! I have an important announcement regarding some upcoming changes to rewards for *Aww, Feathers!* patrons. Here's a quick summary:

- These changes will take place starting July 1, 2021. You have until then to make any desired updates to your pledge level.
- Starting July 1st, new *Aww, Feathers!* Patreon tiers will be introduced. Many of the rewards for these tiers will be the same, but there are a few new ones as well. Read below for more details.
- Existing patrons before July 1st will be able to keep their current pledge level for as long as they maintain their subscription. The old tiers will be marked as "Legacy" tiers, and will no longer be available for new patrons.
- Due to some challenges with shipping, physical *Inked Feathers* drawings will no longer be mailed to $10 and $15 Legacy tiers. Instead, they will receive access to an exclusive monthly art stream. More details below.

### New Patreon Tiers

The following new patron tiers and benefits will be introduced starting July 1st (more details about what each reward entails can be found further down):

**$2 - Feathered Friend!**

- Early access to new comic pages
- Support the comic

**$5 - Awwthusiast!**

- Behind-the-scenes material
- Comic timelapse videos
- Sketchbook drawings
- Name on comic page (if desired)
- Everything from the "Feathered Friend!" tier

**$10 - Awwrt Lover!**

- Monthly art stream
- Bonus drawing collection
- Custom icon with name
- Everything from the "Awwthusiast!" tier

**$20 - Awwrt Collector!**

- Priority art commissions
- Art credits (redeem for art commissions or original artwork)
- Everything from the "Awwrt Lover!" tier

**$50 - Aspiring Awwrtist!**

- 1-hour private lesson on drawing, writing, or cartooning
- Everything from the "Awwrt Lover!" tier

On July 1st, all existing Patreon tiers will be converted into "Legacy" tiers. Current patrons can keep those tiers for as long as they maintain their subscription, but they will become unavailable to new patrons. (You will also not be able to switch from one Legacy tier to another Legacy tier.)

- The current **Awwthusiast** tier will become the **Awwthusiast! (Legacy)** tier. Patrons in this tier will receive the same benefits as those in the new **Awwthusiast!** tier, but will lock in their current, lower price.
- The current **Inked Feathers: Bronze Member** tier will become the **Awwrt Lover! (Legacy)** tier. Patrons in this tier will receive the same benefits as those in the new **Awwrt Lover! **tier, but will lock in their current, lower price.
- The current **Inked Feathers: Silver Member** and **Inked Feathers: Gold Member** tiers will also become the **Awwrt Lover! (Legacy)** tier. Patrons in this tier will receive the same benefits as those in the new **Awwrt Lover!** tier. Note that these tiers will no longer receive monthly physical drawings in the mail. This is due in part to some difficulties in shipping. However, **Silver** members will receive a one-time bonus art credit, and **Gold** members will receive a one-time bonus of two art credits. If you wish to earn more art credits, you can do so by joining the new **Awwrt Collector!** tier starting July 1st. (More details about how to earn and redeem art credits can be found below.)
- *IMPORTANT NOTE!!* If you are currently donating without a reward tier but would like access to one of the Legacy tiers, you must switch to one before July 1st!

### Reward Details

- **Early access - **Each new comic page will be posted here on Patreon several days before it goes public on the main website. Come here to see it first!
- **Support the comic - **It's true. Your support makes *Aww, Feathers!* possible. And the more who join, the more time I'll be able to dedicate towards making comics. So take satisfaction in the fact that you're helping to make it happen. Thank you!
- **Behind-the-scenes material - **Each comic page will also come with a rough draft or other work-in-progress images of the page, as well as some brief commentary on the creation of that page. You'll also get to see the comic script for each chapter, deleted scenes from the comic, and even some bonus scenes that add more detail to the story!
- **Comic timelapse videos - **Watch each comic page take form before your very eyes with a timelapse video, showing everything from text and panel layout to rough drawings, inks, and colors!
- **Sketchbook drawings - **Sometimes I practice drawing a specific subject, experiment with different styles, or even just doodle. Either way, whenever I fill a page of my sketchbook, I share it here and you can see the full page.
- **Name on comic page - **If desired, your name or screen name will be included on the main website underneath each comic page that you supported (meaning you were a patron when that page first came out) as well as on the website's Thank You page. By default, the name listed for you on Patreon will be used. If you wish to use a different name, or to remain anonymous, please message me after joining the tier. You can also message me if you ever wish to change or remove your name in the future. (All chosen names must be G-rated.)
- **Monthly art stream - **Once per month I will hold an art stream, exclusive to those at the appropriate tier. At each stream I will do several drawings based around a chosen theme which will be announced ahead of time. Those in attendance can offer suggestions for what to draw. Some of these will be physical drawings, while others will be digital. (Note, these aren't for sketch requests of your character. For that, see the *Art credits* reward below.) I will make an effort to stream at a time when the most people will be able to attend, and to change it up month to month so that those unable to attend one stream will be able to attend the next. If you know you won't be able to attend a given art stream, you can message me with your drawing suggestions. Videos of each stream will be available for those who missed it, or who would like to rewatch it.
- **Bonus drawing collection - **You'll have access to an online collection of bonus drawings, including those created during monthly art streams and all previous entries in the *Inked Feathers* drawing collection.
- **Custom icon with name - **If you've chosen to have your name included beneath each comic page on the main website, you may also include a small custom icon with your name. The icon should be an image you have permission to use and must be G-rated. (If you'd like me to make one for you, I can do so for a fee.) By default, the icon used for your Patreon profile will be used. If you wish to use a different one, please message me after joining the tier.
- **Priority art commissions - **Each month I will have a limited number of art commission slots available. Patrons at the appropriate level will have priority access to these slots before they are made available to the public.
- **Art credits - **For those who would like to receive a custom art commission or piece of original artwork from me on a regular basis, you can accumulate "art credits". Every month, those at the appropriate tier will earn one credit. These credits roll over month to month and don't expire. When you're ready to redeem credits, simply message me with the type of art commission or the specific drawing you would like. More complex art commissions cost more credits. After receiving your request, I will message you back the exact number of credits it will cost, but below is a basic idea of what you can get:
- An original *Inked Feathers* drawing (1 credit)
- Physical pen and ink commission (2-3 credits)
- Digital sketch commission (1-2 credits)
- Digital colored commission (2-4 credits)
- Digital sticker commission (2 credits)
- Cameo in a future comic page (2 credits)
- Got something else in mind? Just ask!
- **Private lesson - **Would you like a private lesson from me on drawing, writing, or cartooning? Want to do a deep dive on something you created and explore what's working and what can be improved? Sign up for a one-hour lesson with me and I'll share what I've learned from my own years of experience creating comics and artwork. If you miss your lesson one month, don't worry! They don't expire! We'll set up a time that works best for you, even if it's after the month that you pledged.

### Questions?

That's a lot to take in! Do you have any questions about this change? I want to be transparent and also give every advantage possible to those who are among my earliest supporters! So please, if anything doesn't make sense or if you need help making any changes, just let me know in a comment or private message!

Thanks!
-Lark
