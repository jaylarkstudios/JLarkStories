 I'm still having some pain, though the frequency and intensity seems to be going down overall. I still haven't had the energy to complete a full page, but I thought we could do something fun in the interim. ^_^
I've prepared these three mini cartoons with the dialogue missing, and I want you to fill it in! 

 1. Download the ones you'd like to do (right-click and "Save Image As"). You may do one, two, or all three!
2. Come up with dialogue to fill the empty word bubbles. Make sure it's appropriate for all ages!
3. Use whatever method you wish to add your dialogue to the bubbles. Photoshop, MS Paint, or even printing it out and writing it by hand are all acceptable.
4. Submit your entries by 3/24/18 either via email (awwfeathers@gmail.com), share them on twitter and tag @awwfeathers, or share them as photos on the Aww, Feathers! facebook page. Favorites will be displayed on the website!
