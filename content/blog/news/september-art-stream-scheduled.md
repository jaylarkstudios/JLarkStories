The date for the September art stream for [Awwrt Lovers](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) has been set for **Wednesday, September 8th at ****[7pm MT](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/usa/utah)**. A link will be posted on Patreon the day of the stream. The theme will be Villains! Have any ideas for which character from *Aww, Feathers!* you think would fit  as your favorite villain? Could be from a Disney film, comic  book, video game, or any other medium. Bring your suggestions to the stream! The video will be available for those who'd like to watch it after the stream ends, and the resulting images from the stream will be posted on Patreon throughout the month.

If you would like to participate, be sure you’ve joined the [Awwrt Lovers](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) tier and mark your calendar! You'll also gain access to all previous drawings in the Inked Feathers collection, as well as behind-the-scenes and other bonus content for the comic! See you then!
