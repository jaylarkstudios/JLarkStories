If you can read this, that means you're patron level entitles you to make requests for things for me to sketch during my daily drawing practice which will be posted here. Each patron at this level is guaranteed to have at least one of their requests drawn per month. Your request can be as specific or vague as you like. You may request an anthro sketch with a certain species, gender, expression, body pose, prop, etc. Or it can even be non-anthro. Want me to draw a car, a building, a tree, a... space shuttle? Don't be shy! I can benefit from sketching a variety of things, so don't be afraid to be creative!

To make a request, please message me on Patreon or leave a comment on this post or any of my sketchbook posts. And let me know if you have any questions. Thanks again for your support!
