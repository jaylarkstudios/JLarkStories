Get new comic updates early, as well as the knowledge that you're helping to support the comic, for as little as $2 per month! Or check out the other tiers for more bonuses, including behind the scenes material, exclusive *Aww, Feathers!* themed artwork, and even the ability to make a cameo appearance in the comic! If you haven't already, you can join now at [join.awwfeathers.com](http://join.awwfeathers.com)! We've got a spot ready for you!

Thanks all! See you next week!
