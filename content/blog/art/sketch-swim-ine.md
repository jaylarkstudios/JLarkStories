Ine spent a few years on his high school's swim team, partly because it got him out of gym class, but partly because he enjoys swimming. He's a water lizard after all, inherited from his mother's side. He was never the fastest swimmer. But he enjoyed practicing, participating in events, and seeing personal improvement. He hasn't kept up his swimming as much as he'd have liked since graduating high school, serving his mission, and then starting college. But he still enjoys going for an occasional swim.
