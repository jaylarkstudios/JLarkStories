Sudden piece of vent art, just because today was a stressful day. Random coincidence that it fell on the last day of the year. But then again, this was also a stressful year. For me, the middle was okay. But the beginning and the end were very hard for various reasons. But I'll be all right.

One of the most valuable life skills I've developed is the ability to say "I'm not okay right now. But I will be."

And I'm determined to be.

Whatever it takes.
