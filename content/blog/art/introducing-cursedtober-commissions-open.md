It's that time once again… Time for my annual October drawing challenge! This year's has a more overtly spooky theme: cursed objects! Imagine visiting a strange antique shop and buying, say, a grandfather clock. Unbeknownst to you however, the clock is cursed. What happens when you bring it home? What about a cursed mirror? A cursed pair of shoes? A cursed umbrella?? My challenge, to those so inclined, is to draw an image for any (or all) of the provided prompts, depicting the cursed object in question. You could draw just the object itself, or the effects of somebody using it. Whatever you do, just get spooky and share some cursed objects!

This drawing challenge is open to all. As in previous years, I'm also taking pen & ink commissions for October! These will be artistic freedom: the image will include the character and cursed item of your choice, but will otherwise be a spooky surprise! (All finished artwork will be SFW.) These will be $25 USD each, plus $5 If you'd like the originals shipped to you (US only). However, patrons at the *Awwrt Lover* level or higher get their first slot for only $10! [Join the community](http://join.awwfeathers.com/awwrtlover) to take advantage of this deal, and unlock the entire *Inked Feathers* collection, and hundreds of other exclusive posts!

Slots are limited this year; I won't be doing all 31 days. So reserve yours now while you can! Message me or leave a comment to claim one!
