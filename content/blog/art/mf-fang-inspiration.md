Fang presents an interesting challenge. Being a skeleton, I need to draw each individual bone. But I don't want to over-complicate the design by having all the same bones that would exist in a real skeleton. So for example, he may only have three ribs, and much fewer vertebrae and teeth, etc. Clipart is an excellent resource for looking for ways to simplify the shapes and designs of a character.
