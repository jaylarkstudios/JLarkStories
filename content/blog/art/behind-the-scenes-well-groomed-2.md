I made an effort to do more with the typography in this chapter. That includes using different fonts for different sounds (like Ine shivering in the second panel and the rattle sound effect in the third) as well as being more aware of how text elements fit in with the composition of each frame (note the significant change in size and position of the text in the third panel). I also employed a trick I learned not long ago when adding text to an actual object in the scene, such as the sign in the steam room for instance. Instead of using actual text, I traced over the text with my pencil tool. It makes it feel more integrated into the scene, as opposed to dialog and sound effects which are understood to be abstract and not really "there".
