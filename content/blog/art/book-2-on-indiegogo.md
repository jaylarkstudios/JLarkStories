My second book is currently up on Indiegogo! You can back it by visiting [AwwFeathersBook.com](http://awwfeathersbook.com/)! You can also snag a copy of Book 1 if you missed it the first time, as well as some exclusive art prints and other fun perks! This is one of the best ways you can support *Aww, Feathers!* so if you've enjoyed the comic, please consider backing the book today!

Another thing you can do to help is spread the word. Sharing or retweeting posts from [twitter](http://twitter.com/awwfeathers), [facebook](http://facebook.com/AwwFeathers), or [instagram](http://instagram.com/awwfeathers)really help increase visibility. In fact, there's a giveaway going on at the same time as the campaign. You can win a free art commission from me by entering the giveaway here: [https://gleam.io/QHqcz/aww-feathers-book-2-art-giveaway](https://gleam.io/QHqcz/aww-feathers-book-2-art-giveaway)

Thanks so much for your support! Remember to check out [AwwFeathersBook.com](http://awwfeathersbook.com/) to support the book! We're really close to our goal, but after that there are stretch goals to make the book even better! Also, the success of this campaign is one of the things that will, in large part, determine how much time I am able to dedicate to the comic in the near future, so in addition to backing the comic on Patreon, this is a great way to support future comic updates!

Cheers!

-Josh (Lark)
