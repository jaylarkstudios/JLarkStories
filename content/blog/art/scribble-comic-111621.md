I wear glasses while working at the computer, both at home and my day job. They recently renewed my prescription, but the new lenses are darker than before; they've got that yellowish tint that reduces blue light. I was worried they might interfere with my ability to perceive colors while creating art, but decided to give them a try for a few days at least. So far it hasn't been an issue. And besides, I think they look kinda cool. 🤓
