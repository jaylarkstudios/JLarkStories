It was pure chance that Mephi the skunk and Nat the dragon first bumped into each other. What they didn't leave up to chance though was being open and honest with each other. Mephi wears diapers for incontinence, but he also enjoys wearing them, and even though he's male, he often feels more comfortable in a dress. Nat wears diapers for medical reasons, and though she was born female and most often presents that way, she has the ability to transform, and sometimes feels like being a different gender for the day. What might have seemed strange to some only endeared themselves to each other, and the more they've opened up to one another, the closer they've become. For this pic, Nat took on a more androgenous form to represent the more non-binary aspects of her personality, and the two are in their pamps to demonstrate their closeness and acceptance of one another. May everyone one day find a partner with whom they can truly be themselves. 💞
