If this book custody battle doesn't tear their friendship apart, it just might tear the book apart...

Did you ever go halvsies with one of your siblings on a new book that just came out that both of you really wanted to read... and then had to negotiate who would get to read it when and the penalties for spoiling any part of the story? I imagine that's probably what June and her twin would go through whenever a new DinoKin book came out!
