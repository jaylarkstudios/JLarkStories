I finished the chapter on drawing equine characters. They *clearly* saved the best for last: an ice cream sundae alicorn! 😂 My daughter saw it, and I think she wants it to be her new fursona.

The next chapter is about drawing rodent characters! And I will say that most of my characters have relatively long muzzles, so it's a bit of a paradigm shift to draw critters with shorter muzzles. Even that chipmunk I drew in the lower right corner is probably a little longer in the face than he needs to be. I'll work on that. 😉
