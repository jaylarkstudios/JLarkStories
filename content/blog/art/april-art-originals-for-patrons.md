Greetings! I hope that you're doing well.

Patrons may have noticed that my gallery of pen and ink drawings, *Inked Feathers*, has slowed down a bit in recent months. This is due in part both to the extra work I've been putting in to rebuilding the *Aww, Feathers!* website from scratch, and stresses from the current pandemic. I'm sorry for the slowdown, both because it's a useful and valuable exercise for me as an artist to hone my skills and encourage me to produce finished art pieces more regularly, but also because it's one of the promised benefits to you for your Patreon subscription.

Things are getting better though. While there's still plenty I'd like to do with it, the new website is fully operational. There's still plenty of uncertainty over the pandemic of course, but I feel like things have at least settled to the point where I can more effectively mange things that come up.

To make up for the month of April, which didn't have any *Inked Feathers* drawings at all, I wanted to share a few drawings from another project I do on the side called *[Sour Peppers](http://sourpeppers.com)*, which includes uplifting, spiritual, and religious themed drawings and comics. The images above were inked by hand and then colored digitally and put on the site. I've decided to make the inked originals available to patrons who are owed an art original for the month of April. Because they're religious in nature, I'll only send them to those who specifically request one.

Long story short, if you were a $10 patron during the month of April, feel free to pick from the [Available](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=Available) drawings, including those shown above. If you don't specify, one will be picked for you, but the *Sour Peppers* drawings will be left out unless you specifically request one. Please let me know by the end of the month if you have any specific requests. As mentioned before, I'll be holding on to any drawings you are owed until I'm more confident they can be sent safely, unless you ask me to send them to you now. The State of Utah is transitioning to a "low risk" phase of the pandemic, so I expect to be sending them out all together for the month of June. As always, I'll keep you informed.

Thanks again for your support!
-Lark
