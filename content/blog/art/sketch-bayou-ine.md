This originally started as an attempt to draw Ine in the same style as Chalchiutlicue, or Calamity, from the animated series No Evil on betsydraws.com, which is a super cool series by the way! Anyway, I think I basically captured the look and feel, as well as the overall body shape. Overall... ha! Yeah, I don't know why I went with that clothing choice. It just felt right. That and imagining him out fishing in the bayou, like a scaly version of Huck Finn. Maybe Calamity's accent has something to do with it. I hope that's not insensitive; it certainly isn't meant to be! Calamity is my favorite character in the series! Her voice, appearance, abilities... and the fact that she's a lizard, teehee! I wonder if she and Bayou Ine would be buds.
