Going back to following the book for a bit more practice before trying my own characters again. I think that maybe my mistake last time was trying to draw too closely to the reference photo I was using. References are useful, but trying to apply an entirely different style to one without changing the body structure, pose, etc. can be problematic. Take a look at the cartoony critters I've been practicing over the past few days, for instance. Most of them have bodies that don't make much sense in real life, and yet they still appear logical.
