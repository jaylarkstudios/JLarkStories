My girlfriend took me to "Color Me Mine" last week on a date. It's a place that has a bunch of unfired ceramic items that you can then paint up however you want, and then they'll fire them for you. Pretty neat concept!
So I painted Ine catching some chocolate or something on his tongue, and June lol-ing at him. ![:D](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/b/biggrin.gif) June is my girlfriend's character, a dolphin.
I like it a lot! It was a lot of fun to make, and is special because of who I made it with. <3

Okay, so I and my art may be a bit sappy from time to time over the next while; though I don't apologize for my feelings, I hope it doesn't get annoying. Please be patient if it does.
