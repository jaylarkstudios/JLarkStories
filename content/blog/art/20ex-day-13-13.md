I did do my drawing exercises every day, they just didn't get posted every day. And that might happen occasionally. So here's a bit of catch up for the past few days.

It's difficult to find good artistic references for studying how an anthro lizard's pants would accommodate his tail! That's what I attempted to draw for today's exercise though. I found some anthro reptile refs and then tried to draw how they would wear pants. I imagine Ine with the style of jeans with a strap that goes over the top of his tail and then snaps to the other side, like the top right sketch above. That way, he doesn't have to try and thread his tail through the hole every morning; he just pulls his pants up, then zips and snaps them in the front, then snaps them in the back. For those species who, like him, have a tail that's rather thick at the base, that's probably a common style.
