I'm sure Miara is speaking for everyone watching this space right about now. 😅

Joking aside though, I did get some comic work done over the weekend, and in the process have planned out the rest of the progress needed to finish up the next chapter. I'm happy to announce that it's planned to begin the first week of August! 😁

While the break between the previous chapter and this next one won't be as long as the previous break (thank goodness...) it did turn out to be longer than expected. There are numerous reasons for that, not least of which was a recent death in our extended family. Suffice to say though, we are doing well. Things are returning to some semblance of normalcy. Moreover, I'm ready to get back to work on *Aww, Feathers!* once again and have a plan in place to finish the next chapter, as well as the one following that (and so on and so forth).

Thanks for reading! Look forward to the next chapter come August, and in the meantime keep watching this space for more stuff!
