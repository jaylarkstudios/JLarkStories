You'll notice a few places in rough drafts that I've begun dropping in clip art or other "pre-made" visual elements. I use these as place holders, usually only for background elements. On this chapter for example, I use them to take the place of silhouetted figures during the rough draft stage, and then when it comes time to ink, I draw over the top of them. Obviously, the ones I used in this page wouldn't fit as is because they're human figures. But even if they were anthro, I would still take down the opacity, draw over the top of them, and then hide the original. Even if I used the exact same pose, outfit, etc. this is still a useful practice, because it unifies the object with the rest of the scene so it no longer looks "pasted in."
