I really, really like this one! Maybe it's just my own bias, but I feel like, without knowing much about Kraal's hypothetical sister, we learn a lot about their relationship from this one image. They're both quiet and reserved, yet they're also "in sync" with each other on some level.

Oh, and about the name. It's been mentioned before that Kraal has a traditional eagle name (he's not an eagle, but his mother is). Thraa is another one of those names. You'll notice that both names can be spoken loudly and high-pitched to resemble the cry of a bird of prey. With practice, that is. (Please be courteous about where you practice said bird-cries.)
