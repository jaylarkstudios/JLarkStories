"Oh, Holy Night!
The stars are brightly shining,
It is the night of our dear Savior's birth!"

Inked Feathers is back! This month is, of course, Christmas themed! Today's is a tribute to one of my favorite actors, Kristin Chenoweth, and combines her role as Princess Skystar from the *My Little Pony Movie* and her performance of *Oh Holy Night* at the 2018 Christmas concert with the Tabernacle Choir and Temple Square Orchestra! It represents both her bubbling and energetic personality and her deep faith. 💖✝🎄
