Drawing people is hard, okay? 😅 Nah, I've touched on this before: it feels easier to draw cartoon animals, because the only real judge of what they're "supposed" to look like, is you. Still, it's valuable to expand one's skills, and going through Chris Hart's book on human anatomy is stretching mine. I've already seen some ways in which increasing my understanding of human anatomy has carried over to my furry drawings too, so I'm game to keep going!
