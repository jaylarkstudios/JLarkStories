Last year I successfully completed one drawing for every [2019 Inktober](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers?filters[tag]=Inktober) prompt. Since then, I've been toying with the idea of creating my own prompts for 2020. I now give you OCtober, the daily original character creation challenge! To be clear, I'm not the first to think of the name "OCtober" for a series of daily art prompts involving drawing original characters. While others are about drawing OC's in different situations, locations, outfits, etc. my specific angle is drawing a *different* original character to go with each prompt.

By now there are dozens of October art prompts out there, so if you'd like to stretch your creative muscles, pick whichever one intrigues you. If you're so inclined, you are of course more than welcome to give OCtober a try with the prompts I've provided here! Feel free to download and share the above image when you do!

In addition to sharing this set of prompts with anyone who'd like to use them, I'll be going through them myself during the month of October! That brings me to my second announcement: **I'm officially open for OCtober commissions!** These will be black and white, pen-and-ink drawings, comparable to the rest of my [Inked Feathers](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=InkedFeathersPreview) collection in quality and complexity. Color can be added digitally for an additional fee. The subject must be SFW and each must feature a single character who is either (a) from the comic *Aww, Feathers!*, (b) your own creation, or (c) created by a friend, if the drawing intended as a gift. Because the challenge is to draw a *different* character for each day, each character may only be drawn once. (So if there's a character from the comic you'd like to pair with a particular prompt, be sure to snag it quickly!)

These are open to both patrons and non-patrons alike, though patrons get special pricing (details below)! If you're not currently a patron but you subscribe during the month of October, you will be entitled to the same deal, *plus* all the other benefits that come with your membership, including behind-the-scenes posts, the entire Inked Feathers collection, and more!
