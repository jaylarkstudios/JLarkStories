I attempted to use some of the techniques in *Drawing Animals Shape by Shape* to draw some of my own reptilian critters. I can come up with basic shapes to represent the different body parts, but I still need some practice (1) iconifying those shapes to simplify and "cartoonify" the character, and (2) putting those pieces together in a way that both makes sense and is visually appealing.
