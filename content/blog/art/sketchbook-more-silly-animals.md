And that's the last of them! I've officially drawn every animal in Chris Hart's "Drawing Animals Shape by Shape" book! (Good thing to. The book is due back at the library today, haha!) It was a fun exercise. I'll try to apply the skills I picked up in the process to my own artwork to vary the body shapes and sizes of my characters a bit more.

Would I recommend this book? It's certainly a fun one, and probably an excellent way for someone to break into drawing who's less than familiar or comfortable with it. For me though, the real test is how well I can apply the principles the book tries to teach to my own artwork. I'll give it a try and report back!
