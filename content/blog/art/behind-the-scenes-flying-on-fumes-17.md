This is about the time when I finally started getting the hang of my new comic work and release schedule. I've been settling into my new home office and routine. It's still a lot of work, and sometimes hectic. But with each new page that gets completed, I figure out some new way of making the process go a little more smoothly.

That process has changed a ton over the years. And I'm sure it will change again. And that's okay. It's hard to stay balanced when you're standing still; it takes near constant movement and correction as we adjust to our circumstances. The key is to not over-correct, but rather strive to carefully introduce those corrections in a way that will be sustainable.
