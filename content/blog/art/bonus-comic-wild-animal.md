What does the fox say? She says thank you to all the kind souls who help animals in need! 🦊♥

Taking a quick break from guest comics to remind you that the animal sanctuary and education facility, Wild Wonders, is in need of help! Here are a few ways you can serve these critters and those who take care of them:

- Subscribe to *[Aww, Feathers!](http://join.awwfeathers.com)*[ on Patreon](http://join.awwfeathers.com) this month and 100% of your contribution will be donated to Wild Wonders! See [this post](http://patreon.com/posts/37961064) for more details.
- If you prefer, you can donate to them directly at [wildwondersedu.com](http://wildwondersedu.com).
- Purchase something from the Wild Wonders [amazon wishlist](https://smile.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/2ZJRXEIQSMNWR/ref=cm_go_nav_hz) of needed items.
- Select Wild Wonders as your Amazon Smile charity. At no extra cost to you, whenever you shop at smile.amazon.com, a percentage of your order's total will be donated the charity of your choice! Simply log in to your amazon account, go to [this link](https://smile.amazon.com/gp/chpf/change/), then search for "Wild Wonders" and it should be near the top of the list (it's the one located in Genola, Utah.
- Simply share this post or the Wild Wonders website with others to spread the word!
