I was recently commissioned to draw a character portrait with lots of fire in the background, so I took a brief break form my online drawing course to do a study on fire! I have this book I picked up as an animation student and read the chapter on fire, smoke, and explosions. The book is called *Elemental Magic*. Sadly it does not teach you to be a firebender. But it does help you understand the fundamental forces behind different visual effects, including fire, water, wind, etc. And a little bit of knowledge goes a long way!
