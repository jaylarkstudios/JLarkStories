Gender: Male

Species: Dalmatian

Personality: Self-proclaimed "jack-of-all-sports," he enjoys watching and participating in a wide variety of competitive athletic activities. While quite good at several, basketball is one of his favorites. He also enjoys cooking for his friends and roommates. Outgoing and friendly, though sometimes easily annoyed. Fiercely loyal to his friends, and his favorite sports teams. Gregarious and social; would much rather do stuff with friends than by himself.

History: When Chris was fourteen his family took the missionary discussions and was baptized. Growing up, Chris loved to play basketball, but chose to serve a mission instead of trying out for a college team. He's sometimes told that he could have been very good if he had dedicated himself to his training, and while he admits it was a sacrifice, he always says it was the right decision.

----

Chris is roommates with Kraal and rounds out the four characters of Aww, Feathers! who live in number 20 of Apple Orchard Apartments. He has the distinction of being the only among them who doesn't have feathers. 😛 He was also the hardest for me to draw when I was first starting out with the comic. 😅
