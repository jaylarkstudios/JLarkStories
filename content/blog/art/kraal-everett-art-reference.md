Species: Gyrfalcon

Personality: Intelligent but quiet; dislikes a lot of attention. Thoughtful and polite. He usually prefers to be alone, but enjoys the company of others on occasion. Enjoys tabletop and board games when he can find the time to play them. Insightful and willing to offer advice to those who need it when asked. Very private, and doesn't talk much about his personal life.

History: Kraal grew up in a traditional avian home. He's the only child of his widowed mother; his dad was killed while on active duty in the Air Force while Kraal was quite young. While LDS, he hasn't been to Church in years, but he's on friendly terms with the bishop and other members in his ward. He's double-majoring in math and finance, which takes up most of his spare time.Kraal shares a room with Chris the dalmatian, and together they share an apartment with Ine and Dil. While Kraal and Chris are very different in personality, they've developed a respect for each other.
