Been a while since I've uploaded a sketchbook page! I left off on drawing the masculine figure from the back, and now it's on to the feminine figure. Once again, male and female characters can be drawn in a variety of ways, and they're all beautiful. This book emphasizes the traditional "idealized" human figure, and therefore focuses on things like the the curve of the back, the tapering of the waist, the wideness in the hips, etc. I would love to do an anatomy study that explores more variety of the human figure, but this is probably a decent place to start.
