Hummingbirds are smarter than you think. They have to calculate the optimal schedule for visiting flowers in order to maximize nectar collection. They need that nectar in order to give them energy to fly. And obviously they need to be able to fly in order to... collect nectar... um... Let's not think about that too much...

I felt like doing a species swap, so I randomly picked two characters, and Mally and Kraal's name came up! Hey, who knows? Maybe in another universe things worked out between them and they got married and had a kid who grew up to look like this guy. I realize now I should have spelled it "Mraal." Ah well.

Just don't ask how those glasses stay perched on his beak...
