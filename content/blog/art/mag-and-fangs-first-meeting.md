This is a test scene I've put together with both Mag and Fang in order to test out their size difference, the line quality I want, and the coloring style I hope to use.

"Mag & Fang" (working title) is a story about a magpie nature witch who spends her days taking care of the forest she lives in. During an attempt to create a helper for her, she accidentally reanimates the skeleton of a raptor that, unbeknownst to her, was buried nearby. While friendly and eager to "help", the raptor ends up being more of a handful than she could have anticipated!

This is the short story I've been developing over the past month, and that I'll be working on for the remainder of this year! I've had a lot of fun developing the story and characters, and I look forward to bringing them to the page!

Check out my webcomic, Aww, Feathers! at [https://awwfeathers.com](https://awwfeathers.com)
