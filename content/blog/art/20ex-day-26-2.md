I've already raved quite a bit about line-of-action.com, but seriously, it's a very handy tool. In addition to references for gesture drawing, animals, hands, and feet, they also have references for drawing facial expressions, which is what I used today. Man, the head tilted up pose is a very tricky one for me...

Strictly speaking, this isn't exactly what exercise #2 says (draw the six basic facial expressions). But it's the closest thing to what I actually did. In the near future I'll probably refine my list of drawing exercises in order to focus on the things I've identified as most important for me to work on.
