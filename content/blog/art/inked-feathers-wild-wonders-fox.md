This month's Inked Feathers drawings will features some of those in the care of  [Wild Wonders](http://wildwondersedu.com/) , an animal sanctuary and education facility! They take care of many animals in need, including those undergoing rehabilitation and those who can't survive in the wild. Today's featured animal is Juno, the marbled phase red fox! She's an animal ambassador of Wild Wonders. I had the opportunity to meet Juno when Wild Wonders came to Anthro Weekend Utah (AWU) last year. That was a really neat experience! She's very chill and totally down for pets and scritches.

The bad news is that Wild Wonders has had to cancel a number of fundraising events this year due to COVID-19, including returning to AWU this year. They and the animals they care for are in need of help. So during the month of June, all proceeds form *Aww, Feathers!* on Patreon will be donated to Wild Wonders, including contributions from all new and existing patrons. (I'll cover any payment processing costs, so 100% of your contribution amount will go to Wild Wonders.) As thanks, you'll get to enjoy a month of exclusive *Aww, Feathers!* content, including the entire collection of Inked Feathers drawings, like the one above and the rest that will be revealed throughout this month! If you're not currently a patron, you can [subscribe here](http://join.awwfeathers.com) to get your bonus content and to help animals in need!

If you prefer to donate to Wild Wonders directly, you can do so at [wildwondersedu.com](http://wildwondersedu.com).

Thank you!
