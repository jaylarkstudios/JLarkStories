Viola! He is finished! >:3
Well, I once heard that 90% of the time you spend on a project is spent on the last 10% of said project, and I think that came pretty close to being true on this one! (Lighting and texturing are *hard*, man!) I couldn't get the ground to quite look the way I wanted, but overall I'm very happy with it!
This was my final project for my 3D Graphics class, and the 3D version of [this guy](/blog/art/rrougath/).
If you compare the two, you'll noticed I changed his colors and also swapped those appendages beneath his neck for real spider-like mandibles in his mouth! 🦷🦷
I'm proud of it; Rrougath was 100% my own creation, from concept to final product! (This has done quite a bit for my self-confidence!)

Pleased as I am with it, I would still love to hear any suggestions on how it might be improved, because I'm sure it still has a long way to go. Enjoy!
