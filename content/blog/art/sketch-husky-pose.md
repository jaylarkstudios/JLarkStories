Anthro Weekend Utah (AWU) was great! I had a wonderful time with friends, met a lot of people who came by my table, and also attended several great panels. One of which was Silvixen's fursuit life drawing. She and another fursuiter (Evo Husky, I believe) did poses for us while we drew them. We did several in different time increments. I thought my fifteen minute sketch turned out pretty good, so I decided to ink it. It was a fun exercise!
