I intend to continue doing practice sketches each day, but I'm taking a break from my list of 20 character drawing exercises. That is, I won't be sticking to it as rigidly. It served me well, but I'll be coming up with a new list for myself now that I have a better idea of where my strengths and weaknesses lie. For now, I dipped a bit into Christopher Hart's book, *Simplified Anatomy for the Comic Book Artist*. The first few pages take a look at how the skull defines the shape of the face, and how we associate different skull shapes with different characteristics: masculine, feminine, heroic, sinister, intelligent, brutish, etc.

And a reminder, I'm curious to know how you like receiving these daily drawing exercises. Please let me know in this quick, 1-question poll **[here](https://www.patreon.com/posts/drawing-exercise-23212071)**.
