Who wants to test a game I'm making? :D Here's a special opportunity available to you and other top supporters before it goes out to a wider audience!

A few years ago I made a prototype for a silly game called "Poke the Dragon!" The gameplay was a little rough around the edges, and the artwork even more so. But a lot of people said it was surprisingly fun! And then it got put back on the shelf as other projects took priority…

I'm excited to announce however that I've dusted it off and spruced it up quite a bit! My goal is to create and actually publish a simple app that I can then add to my portfolio to show off to potential clients/employers and demonstrate my skills as a programmer and app and game developer!

The game has come a long way in a very short time since I started working on it in earnest. It's nearly ready for an Alpha release! If you would be interested in testing the alpha and giving your feedback, please follow the instructions below. It's a pretty simple, casual mobile game, so even if you don't consider yourself a "gamer," I'd still love for you to give it a try and offer your honest feedback!

Your comments are welcome, but if you are actually interested in testing, PLEASE follow all the instructions below! If you don't, I won't be able to send you an invite!

Instructions:
1. You will need an Android device that can install apps from the Google Play Store.
2. You will also need a Google account and the email address associated with that account.
3. Send an email to **testing@jaylarkstudios.com** with your name, the email address you use to log in to the Google Play Store, and your desire to help me test the game. (Just a simple, "I want to help test Poke the Dragon!" is sufficient.)
4. Please add **testing@jaylarkstudios.com** to your contact list to ensure you can receive further instructions and updates from me.

You should receive an email at the address you provided within a few days with a link to download the first version of the app, as well as instructions on what to look for and how to submit your feedback!

If you do not own an Android device but are still interested in testing, please email **testing@jaylarkstudios.com** to let me know what kind of device you have, and I'll notify you when/if it becomes available. I do plan on releasing an app for iOS (iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch, etc.) in the near future.

Thanks!
