I'm glad I got to do OCtober number 16! Let me tell you about my imaginary friend I had as a kid! I actually had several, but the one I had longest was an ostrich named Lydia, based on the lead ostrich ballerina in *Dance of the Hours* from Disney's *Fantasia*. To my young mind she was sometimes sort of a mother-like figure, but more often she was a friend who lived in a made-up world and went with me on adventures in said world. It's sure nice to see her again!
