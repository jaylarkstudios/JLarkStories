Look, I'm not going to draw the spots on *every single one*, a'ight?

There is definitely something to be said though for the value of just drawing the same thing over and over again, possibly with some slight variations on each one. The advice I've heard is to "draw it until it stops changing." I don't know if I have *quite* that much time on my hands, haha. But I can still tell that the more I draw one thing, the more the details start to solidify.
