I'm comparing the way I typically draw June's face in profile with some of the new things I want to incorporate into her look. As you can see, there are some things I want to keep and other things I want to fix. I know I keep harping on the shape of a dolphin's jaw, but you just don't realize how odd it is until you sit down to do it. Because on most animals, the lower jaw tucks up underneath the upper jaw. It's the opposite on dolphins (and some other marine animals). I think I've got it down pretty good when her mouth is closed. Mouth open though is causing me problems. Getting there though!
