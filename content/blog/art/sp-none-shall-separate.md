I wanted to let you know about another side project of mine.  *Sour Peppers* is a blog where I post little doodles each week of various one-off comic ideas I've had, as well as some that illustrate scriptures or other religious topics that have special meaning to me. I don't plan to upload all of them to deviantArt, but thought I'd share a few of my favorites.
This is one of my earlier ones, and it's special to me because there have been many times in my life that I've felt burdened by trials or anxiety. During those times, I've prayed often for help. I would say rarely does the pain immediately go away after praying for relief, but I can also say that I've never felt completely abandoned. God has been there, lending me the support I need and the assurance that things will get better. And they always have, with time and patience.

If you'd like to see more of these, please visit [sourpeppers.com](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?http://sourpeppers.com).
