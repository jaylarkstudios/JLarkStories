I played D&D with my siblings over the Thanksgiving break. It was the first time I played in… heck, probably over a decade! And it was my first time playing 5e. I played a halfling ranger with a wolf animal companion. Sadly, he was killed in battle, and none of us had the ability to raise the dead. 😢 If it helps, our DM felt bad about it. After all, we signed up to face the dark denizens of the dungeon, not to lose a faithful canine companion! But it's all right. He fell valiantly and will be remembered. 💙🐺
