Have you heard of Furvilla? I'm not quite sure what it is yet, but apparently it's some sort of social game that's in development. You can find more info about it here: [www.furvilla.com/](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?http://www.furvilla.com/)
Anyway, they have a bunch of anthro characters and have provided uncolored bases that anyone can use to create their own characters. It's unclear whether these user-colored versions will be able to be used in game, or whether this is just for fun, but a bunch of my friends were making them so I thought it would be fun to give it a try as well. So I made Ine of course. ^w^
I didn't quite like the way the velociraptor looked, so I borrowed its head feathers and stuck them on the lizard base, then grabbed the tail feathers from one of the dragons. I nearly always draw Ine clothed, so I wasn't sure how I'd feel about him being all "au naturel". But the bases are a little more "animal" than my usual style, which I think makes it work.
Wanna make your own? You can find the bases and tutorials for how to make them here:  [www.furaffinity.net/user/furvi…](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?http://www.furaffinity.net/user/furvilla/)
