Please feel free to just keep scrolling. I won't be offended. I also think it's horrible. 😅 I was going for Ine's evil twin. What would he be called...? Eni? Sure, let's go with that. I typically draw Ine with a very youthful, sincere looking face. So Eni has a leaner, older looking face, with a sleazy-looking smile that doesn't reach his eyes... He probably hates cricket fudge ice cream and thinks video games are for dweebs. What a jerk! Welp... I apologize in advance to whichever patron is (un)lucky enough to be sent this disasterpiece! 😂
