It's good to be working on comics again!

*It's good to be doing anything that isn't *moving* again!*

*Cue Staler and Waldorf laugh*

Fun fact: This is page number 2^8 of *Aww, Feathers!* What does that mean? It means I'm a huge nerd. 🤓🧮 It also means that I've been working on this comic for a really long time! But you know, every time I take a break from it, I always find myself drawn back. I love telling stories, especially this one in particular. I guess that means you're stuck with me!

We're all moved in to our new house. Unless you've ever done it yourself, you might not believe just how physically, mentally, and emotionally exhausting moving can be. But overall, we were very fortunate. Things worked out very well for us, and while my break was definitely needed, I'm super grateful to finally be able to start drawing again.

Here's to page 2^9! Which will probably take a while, but hopefully not nine more years! 🤞
