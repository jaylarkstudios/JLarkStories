A new character concept. She's a highland cow, named Maggie, or "Nana Mag" to those who her to know her. She's sharp as a whip, but warm as a hearth, and will gladly take in a needy traveler for the night and prepare them a hot meal, so long as they wipe their hooves first!

Mag is in her late 40's or early 50's. I don't draw older characters very often. I think I did a good job on her!
