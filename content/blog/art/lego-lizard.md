The second large project for my Autodesk Maya class was to model a lego dude, texture him, place him in a scene, and render it. This whole thing was done by me! The only reference we were given was a front and side view image of the legs, body and head... we had to figure out the arms by ourselves (and believe me, they were hard!!)
This turned out waaaay better than I had hoped, even though I was a bit rushed at the end. So pleased! :3 I may have to animate him one of these days.

EDIT: I fixed the sky a little bit. That is all.
