If you've been following *Aww, Feathers! *for at least a couple years, you might know I like to have a bit of fun around the beginning of April! Love it or hate it, April Fools Day is an opportunity for me to try something new. This year I collaborated with Celtic Rose, creator of *[Chronicles of Atlum: Cross](http://chroniclesofatlum.thecomicseries.com)** *and *[Black Nostalgia](http://www.blacknostalgia.thecomicseries.com/)*. We did a "panel swap", in which we both chose a scene from the other's comic to draw in our own style. *Cross* is a darker, more serious story than *Aww, Feathers!* but it's an exciting story, and still has a few light-hearted moments. I chose one of those moments for my panel swap, and I must say, I really like the way it turned out!

In particular, I'm very pleased with the colors. I like how the characters stand out from the background. Baggie (the panther) has dark fur, but they're also up against a dark background. I was able to choose colors for both that help her to stand out while still appearing the right color. It was also fun to test out different font choices and panel thicknesses. And I believe this is the first time I've ever drawn a character with a black eye, so there's that!
