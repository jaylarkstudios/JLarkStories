It's America's 250th. But I don't feel like celebrating.
I love my country, but I don't love where it's heading. I don't think America is irredeemable. But I don't think it can redeem itself. Redemption and healing can only come when its leaders and citizens begin living the principals of liberty and justice they espouse. Until then, I pray 251 looks a little bit brighter, and I'll do what I can to help make that happen.
