Continuing my daily character drawing exercises. This time, it's Ine and the six basic emotional expressions. Obviously there are more than just six expressions. But a good explanation I've heard is that just like there are three primary colors that can be combined to create millions of other colors, you can mix these basic expressions together and/or vary their intensity to create other interesting expressions.

If I could improve any in particular, I think it would be fear and surprise. For some reason it's difficult for me to get the eye wide enough to look like he's afraid/surprised. Will keep practicing though.
