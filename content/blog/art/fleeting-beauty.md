Participated in an artist trading card swap, hosted by ~[sunshineley](https://sunshineley.deviantart.com)!  This is the first of three cards.  The theme was "butterflies and blue skies."  All needed to use butterflies and the color blue.  I decided to do mine in pen and ink with a bit of colored pencil.  I had a lot of fun with them!
I'm very happy how the scales turned out, and the hand in general.  I kinda wish I'd done something different with the background, but I guess that's what I get for being impatient... ![:shrug:](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/s/shrug2.gif)
