We blended up some spinach and mixed them with some eggs to make "Green Christmas Eggs" and no, we did not tell the kids. They just assumed we used green food coloring and gobbled it right up.
Technically we didn't lie! We just… allowed them to believe something that's not true in the service of getting them to eat more veggies. Yay parenting!
