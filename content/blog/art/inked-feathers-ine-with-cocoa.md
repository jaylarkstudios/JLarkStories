September has been a bit of a slow month for Inked Feathers, but I realized that it's been a while since I've done a regular, non-stylized drawing of Ine, so I drew him celebrating the beginning of hot cocoa season. I also colored this one digitally to show an example of what you can expect should you order a colored [OCtober commission](http://awwfeathers.com/october) from me. (Since it's colored digitally, you'll get the black and white original if you select this one as your monthly Inked Feathers original reward.)

As a reminder, these Inked Feathers drawings are normally patron exclusives, with the exception of the occasional public post. However, during the month of October, they will all be public, and patrons pledging $5 or more will receive one or more of the drawings as a commission! Again, [see this link](http://awwfeathers.com/october) for more details.

Enjoy the cozy autumn months and enjoy yourselves a nice warm mug of cocoa! ☕
