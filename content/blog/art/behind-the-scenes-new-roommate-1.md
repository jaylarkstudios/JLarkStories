My own apartment number was 20 when I was in a complex for young single adults. It was called Cinnamon Tree though, instead of Apple Orchard. But I mean, "apple cinnamon"? It kinda fits, right? 😋

And here we get our very first look at Dilawar! I was also lucky enough to room with my best friend from high school rather than somebody I didn't even know. To be clear though, Dilawar doesn't directly represent him. In these early pages, many characters had a real world analog. Dil though was one that was almost entirely made up. He quickly became a fan favorite though! Probably because of his unique character design and fun-loving personality.
