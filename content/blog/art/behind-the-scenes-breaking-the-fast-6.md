I just love the subtle interplay between Chris and Adam on this page! Obviously, Adam can tell that Mally is a little peeved with Chris. She may have even told Adam about the drama between Chris and her "best roommate ever". Moreover, he's been dating Mally long enough to know her personality. It seemed appropriate to have him show his approval for the way Chris handled placating her. Small but meaningful interactions like these between characters who otherwise would just be hanging out in the background with nothing else to do can help bring some extra life to a scene.

Oh, and I don't actually know of any recipe for honey hibiscus cookies. But there are honey cookies, as well as hibiscus infused honey. If there were a cookie that was perfectly suited for a bird who needs lots of quick energy to fly and loves to sip flower nectar, I'd say Chris nailed that one, and it seems Mally would agree!
