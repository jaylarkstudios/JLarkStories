Part of a trade I did with Scarbo the Black Sheep. I requested that he create an 8-bit version of one of my favorite Classical pieces, Danse Macabre, and in exchange I'd do a couple art pieces for him. He did a great job on the song by the way! You can listen to it here:  [www.youtube.com/watch?v=KKJJ-q…](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KKJJ-qkvdVU)

Anyway, here's the first part of my end of the trade: his new character, Givette, a white otter gal who's into martial arts! Huah! I'm still figuring out how to draw muscular characters well. I think this one turned out better than some of my other attempts though, which I'm happy for. Oh, and I also had fun doing that training dummy in the background. 😆
