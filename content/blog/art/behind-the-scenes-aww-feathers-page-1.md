Will someone please explain to me why I used to do my rough sketches in such a weird shade of red? 😂

I think I remember at least one time in the dreaded Testing Center when I got partway through a test only to realize I had filled in the bubbles for the wrong questions, and had to completely wear out an eraser in order to fix it!

This page was actually added a year or so after the comic had started. Originally, the story began with the Q&A pages at the very beginning, followed by the first page of the Housing Sitch chapter. I added this new page 1 in preparation for printing the first book. I thought it would be a much better hook than the informative but less attention-grabbing Q&A page format. I think it worked quite well!
