This story took an unexpected turn, right? Who are these people?? Nah,  I'm teasing. For April Fools I did a panel swap with Celtic Rose,  creator of the comic *[Chronicles Of Atlum: Cross](http://chroniclesofatlum.thecomicseries.com)*! I drew one of my  favorite moments from her comic, and she did the same for mine! Go to  her website to see one of her favorite moments from *Aww, Feathers!* rendered in her own style. While you're there, check out *Cross*, which has already finished, so you can read the whole thing! It's a darker  story than mine, but a fun and exciting one! Tell her Lark sent you!
