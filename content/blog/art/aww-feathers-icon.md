More preparation for my upcoming comic, "Aww, Feathers!" This time an icon, to be used for the comic's twitter and facebook pages, featuring Ine looking bashful at all the attention he's receiving.
A couple things you might not have known about Ine (which are design decisions I finally settled on) are that his head-feathers are very tiny and numerous, almost like fur. Also, he has purple stripes on his neck and face, but they are usually not very visible except when he's embarrassed or feeling some other strong emotion; so basically it's his form of blushing!
