What on earth could have elicited a reaction like this from Ine? Is Dil up to his usual shenanigans? Did Chris sign him up for an early morning racquetball class? Did he just see that one girl he's been crushing on and realize he forgot to put on after-shed...? We can only guess.

A while back I mentioned the idea of the *[Aww, Feathers! ](https://www.patreon.com/posts/coming-soon-club-16728264)*[Sketchbook Club](https://www.patreon.com/posts/coming-soon-club-16728264). Now that I'm finally on the mend, I'm ready to start giving that a try. I've got a few fun ideas, including trying out drawing characters in different styles, experimenting with totally new characters, and even some hand drawn mini comics! The first few entries will be made public so you can get an idea of what you'll be in for, but after that they'll be exclusive to patrons who pledge $3 or more. Patrons who pledge $5 or more will be sent one of the original sketches each month, and for $10 or more you may make a request for the sketch you want to see done! I hope you enjoy them!
