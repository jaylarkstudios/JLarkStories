I am entirely too pleased with myself for that third panel! 😂 So what would *her* name be? Romeow and... Poochliet?

It's not hard to imagine the existence of tribalism and conflict between anthros of different species throughout history. As I believe I mentioned before though, I try not to draw too many direct comparisons with similar conflicts in human societies. Difference in species is one thing that people can use to cause divisions, but is by no means meant to reflect any single aspect like race, ethnicity, etc. We're all different in lots of ways. But if I lion and a goat can be friends, then so can we!
