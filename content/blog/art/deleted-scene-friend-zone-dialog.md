The dialog in this scene changed significantly from what I had originally written. The original version left an open loop that I later decided I didn't actually want to have open. I didn't want to keep people wondering if something was ever going to work out between June and Chris. At the same time though I wanted to wrap up the chapter with them on friendly, but not romantic terms. The second version accomplishes this much better. It gives the audience (and June) some closure. June and Chris are "just friends," and once that's made clear, they're both fine with that. They're now both free to seek out other relationships. Not that I'm foreshadowing... probably...
