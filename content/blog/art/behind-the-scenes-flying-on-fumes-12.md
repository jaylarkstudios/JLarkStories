I believe this was the first comic page that I started working on with my new Samsung tablet. I'm not sponsored or anything, but so far I've been a fan. I still use my primary computer to do most of the work. But more than anything, it's just nice to have something I can carry around with me that has Clip Studio already installed on it, so if I ever have some down time I can whip it out and start working on some sketches or coloring.

That's not to say that you need any specific device in order to be creative. I'm a big advocate for using what you have in whatever situation you happen to be in. To this day I still carry around a tiny pocket sketchbook so I always have at least something I can draw on. When it comes to getting a bigger, nicer, more expensive tool, I would actually advocate for waiting on it until you are sure you will make enough use out of it. Because remember, it's not the tool that will make you a good artist. Establish the habit of creating with what you already have first, then a good tool can make that process even easier.
