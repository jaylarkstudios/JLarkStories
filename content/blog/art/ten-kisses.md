Ten Kisses

1. A kiss to commemorate

the day we were wed.

2. A kiss to remember countless

moments of sweetness.

3. A kiss of comfort during

times of grief and heartache.

4. A kiss to savor years of

silliness and laughter.

5. A kiss of gratitude for

endless acts of service.

6. A kiss to celebrate the births

of our beautiful children.

7. A kiss to send my love,

even when we're apart.

8. A kiss to express the passion

you kindle in my heart.

9. A kiss for all the years

we've already been together.

10. A kiss for many,

many more years to come!

Happy 10th Anniversary!A gift for my wife for our 10th anniversary last year. 💓 I left one of these drawings around the house each day for the ten days leading up to our anniversary, then put them among the flowers shown at the bottom for the day itself.

I've loved every day of our life together, and look forward to many, many more to come! 🥰
