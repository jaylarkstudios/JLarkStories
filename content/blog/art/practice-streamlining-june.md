Continuing to practice my "streamlined look." This time on our leading lady dolphin! I keep looking back and forth between the front view and the side view and thinking... hmm... I don't know if that's quite right. But we'll get there.

Key visual features for June are, of course, her dolphin tail and fin, and I'd also say her large eyes. Her figure also, to some extent, though as with my practice page of streamlining Ine's look, much of her figure has been simplified. Still, she's not skinny. I don't think I'd call her especially plump either, but somewhere in between I suppose. Different body types is another thing I definitely want to work on, but in particular how to show it using just a few simple shapes and lines.
