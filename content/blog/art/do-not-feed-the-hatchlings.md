Hydroa learns the hard way why you shouldn't try to feed wild dragon hatchlings. Mama is not pleased...
So, do any of you remember when I offered to do free requests a while back? Like, a loooooooong while back? Yeah, this is one of those. Finally decided I'd make good on it and actually get them done!
Besides, it was good practice; I used MangaStudio to ink this one and then colored it in Photoshop. I actually like the process and may use it more often in the future. I also want to practice creating backgrounds for my art; real backgrounds, not just abstract ones. This one I kept pretty simple, but I think it looks okay.
