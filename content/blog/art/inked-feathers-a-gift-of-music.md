Just to be nice, Lia got her roommate a collection of songs from one of her favorite singers! (But on CD. Who still uses CDs?)

I know we're beginning this month's Inked Feather's theme a bit late. As you can probably guess, my life has been significantly disrupted by the spread of a certain virus. But I'm sure you've already heard enough about that to last a lifetime. So I want to talk about spreading *kindness *instead! Take a moment to think about who in your life could use a little extra kindness and how you can share it with them.
