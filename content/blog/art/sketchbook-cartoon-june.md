When I was experimenting with drawing *Aww, Feathers!* characters in the style of the cartooning book I recently read, I didn't like the way I drew June, so I spent a page focusing on different cartoony styles of her. I like the face on the first one and the waist and hips of the second one. The rest... eh, I can take it or leave it. But that's what experimentation is for!
