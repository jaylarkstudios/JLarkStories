Just a page full of faces, practicing the basic expressions of emotion on the human face. As noted in Scott McCloud's book, *Making Comics*, you can mix and match features from different emotions to get brand new ones:

- worried eyes + smiling mouth = nervous or awkward
- skeptical eyes + half-smiling mouth = contemptuous smirk
- sad eyes + smiling mouth = contemplative or peaceful
- angry eyes + sad mouth = worried or bothered
- etc.
