Keep it simple! Tuesday's exercise was to fill a page with drawings of my characters, done in a simple or minimalistic style. I tried out a variety of ideas. Some turned out kind of fun, and others... didn't really work, haha! But it's interesting to see which lines you can get away with leaving out, and which ones are essential to defining the shape and gesture of the character.
