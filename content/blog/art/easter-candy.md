Ooooh, peanut butter eggs... my kryptonite... Comic delayed on account of Easter Candy. I'm sure we can think of at least a few reasons that makes sense...

No actually, lately I've been working on a two-week schedule, so when I wasn't able to complete a page last week, it meant this week would be a bye week as well. As per yoosh however, I've got a little something for the gap. This one is based on real events, which I'm sure comes as no surprise to those who know about my sweet-tooth. Er... sweet-beak?

Fun fact, this comic actually originated from my wife's idea, so thank her when you see her. 😂 Another fun fact: this one was done traditionally rather than digitally. In fact, it's a public preview of what's available to subscribers to *Aww, Feathers!* on Patreon! There are now over sixty exclusive, *Aww, Feathers!* themed pen and ink drawings available for your viewing pleasure, and for a little extra you can even receive an original drawing (maybe even the one above).

Before I ramble on too much longer, let me say thanks again for your patience. I'm devising a work schedule that will hopefully keep future interruptions to a minimum, but they will still doubtless happen from time to time. I'm finally learning to prioritize my health which ultimately is better for me, my family, and even the comic. So in the long run, it will be worth the occasional gap. Take care, see you next week, and try to steer clear of any lingering discount candy sales!
