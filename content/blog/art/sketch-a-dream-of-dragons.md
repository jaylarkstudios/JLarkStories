I had a dream the other night. A lucid dream in fact. Which doesn't happen to me very often, but whenever it does, the first thing I do is usually to jump into the air and start flying. Eventually though I decided it would be even better if I was flying on the back of a dragon. And I was right! I didn't dream I was Ine though. I don't think I've ever dreamed myself as an anthropomorphic character. When I mentioned this fact to a friend of mine recently, they playfully asked how I could consider myself a true furry. Telling them I do sometimes dream about dragons though seemed to redeem myself.
