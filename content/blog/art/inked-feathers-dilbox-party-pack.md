Not the first time we've seen [Dil in a box](https://www.patreon.com/posts/sketch-dil-in-19683853), but I can think of no one better to represent the quirky and fun collection of virtual party games that belong to the jackbox moniker! Fibbage, Quiplash, Guespionage, Bracketeering, Tee-K-O, Survive the Internet, and Trivia Murder Party are all favorites of myself and the group of friends I frequently play with!
