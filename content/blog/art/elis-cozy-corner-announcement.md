Hey all! For the past few months I've been working on a very special project. I'm launching a new twitch channel called "Eli's Cozy Corner." 💫 It will be focused on soft, soothing vibes, and each stream will end with a bedtime story, either written or improvised by me! My goal is to provide a safe and cozy space to help viewers relax and unwind from their day.

It will be hosted by Eli, my floofy dragon boy. Eli is a very special character to me. He represents my softer, sensitive side. He's shy, but he wants to help you end your day on a pleasant note, and maybe even help you get ready for a good night's sleep. ☺️

Note that this new channel of mine is ABDL/Babyfur friendly. While all content will be SFW, stories and artwork may sometimes feature those themes, and viewers are welcome to discuss them in chat. If that's not your thing, but you can be kind to those simply being themselves, you're welcome to join our cozy crew! 🤗

Check out EliCozyCorner.com to be notified when Eli goes live. See you then!
