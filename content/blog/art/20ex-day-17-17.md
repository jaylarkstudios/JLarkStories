I'm not like, a *huge* fan of Adventure Time, but I do think the art style is fun and whimsical. So I chose that style for Monday's exercise, which was to draw characters in an exaggerated cartoony style. I definitely think this qualifies! I did a couple practice sketches of Finn and Jake, and then drew a few of my own characters in the same style. At first glance, the style can seem chaotic and willy-nilly. But actually studying it and drawing it myself revealed that each line is actually very deliberate and carefully drawn. The characters are made of simple shapes and lines that combine to create something wild and zany!
