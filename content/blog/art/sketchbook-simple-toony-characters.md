As mentioned, when I try out a new art book, the real key to knowing whether I gained significant benefit from it is whether I am able to apply the principles in the book to my own work. So I tried out a few of the techniques from Chris Hart's *Drawing Animals Shape by Shape* book on my own characters. I think they turned out really neat! I especially like how Ine and Chris turned out! Kraal and Mally look okay too. Dil and June didn't really work out the way I hoped, but that's all right. I may do more experimenting in the future with this style!
