### Reward Details

- **Early access - **Each new comic page will be posted here on Patreon several days before it goes public on the main website. Come here to see it first!
- **Support the comic - **It's true. Your support makes *Aww, Feathers!* possible. And the more who join, the more time I'll be able to dedicate towards making comics. So take satisfaction in the fact that you're helping to make it happen. Thank you!
- **Behind-the-scenes material - **Each comic page will also come with a rough draft or other work-in-progress images of the page, as well as some brief commentary on the creation of that page. You'll also get to see the comic script for each chapter, deleted scenes from the comic, and even some bonus scenes that add more detail to the story! Every comic page on the main website will have links to all bonus material related to that page, or you can search for the [Bonus Material](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=BonusMaterial) tag on Patreon.
- **Comic timelapse videos - **Watch each comic page take form before your very eyes with a timelapse video, showing everything from text and panel layout to rough drawings, inks, and colors!
- **Sketchbook drawings - **Sometimes I practice drawing a specific subject, experiment with different styles, or even just doodle. Either way, whenever I fill a page of my sketchbook, I share it here and you can see the full page. You can see them all by searching for the [Sketchbook](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters%5Btag%5D=Sketchbook) tag.
- **Name on comic page - **If desired, your name or screen name will be included on the main website underneath each comic page that you supported (meaning you were a patron when that page first came out) as well as on the website's Thank You page (currently under construction, but once done, your name will be added). By default, the name listed for you on Patreon will be used. If you wish to use a different name, or to remain anonymous, please message me after joining the tier. You can also message me if you ever wish to change or remove your name in the future. (All chosen names must be G-rated.)
- **Monthly art stream - **Once per month I will hold an art stream, exclusive to those at the appropriate tier. At each stream I will do several drawings based around a chosen theme which will be announced ahead of time. Those in attendance can offer suggestions for what to draw. Some of these will be physical drawings, while others will be digital. (Note, these aren't for sketch requests of your character. For that, see the *Art credits* reward below.) I will make an effort to stream at a time when the most people will be able to attend, and to change it up month to month so that those unable to attend one stream will be able to attend the next. If you know you won't be able to attend a given art stream, you can message me with your drawing suggestions. Videos of each stream will be available for those who missed it, or who would like to rewatch it.
- **Bonus drawing collection - **You'll have access to an online collection of bonus drawings, including those created during monthly art streams and all previous entries in the *Inked Feathers* drawing collection. You can see all bonus drawings by searching for the [Inked Feathers](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?filters[tag]=InkedFeathers) tag.
- **Custom icon with name - **If you've chosen to have your name included beneath each comic page on the main website, you may also include a small custom icon with your name. The icon should be an image you have permission to use and must be G-rated. (If you'd like me to make one for you, I can do so for a fee.) By default, the icon used for your Patreon profile will be used. If you wish to use a different one, please message me after joining the tier.
- **Priority art commissions - **Each month I will have a limited number of art commission slots available. Patrons at the appropriate level will have priority access to these slots before they are made available to the public.
- **Art credits - **For those who would like to receive a custom art commission or piece of original artwork from me on a regular basis, you can accumulate "art credits". Every month, those at the appropriate tier will earn one credit. These credits roll over month to month and don't expire. When you're ready to redeem credits, simply message me with the type of art commission or the specific drawing you would like. More complex art commissions cost more credits. After receiving your request, I will message you back the exact number of credits it will cost, but below is a basic idea of what you can get:
- An original *Inked Feathers* drawing (1 credit)
- Physical pen and ink commission (2-3 credits)
- Digital sketch commission (1-2 credits)
- Digital colored commission (2-4 credits)
- Digital sticker commission (2 credits)
- Cameo in a future comic page (2 credits)
- Got something else in mind? Just ask!
- **Private lesson - **Would you like a private lesson from me on drawing, writing, or cartooning? Want to do a deep dive on something you created and explore what's working and what can be improved? Sign up for a one-hour lesson with me and I'll share what I've learned from my own years of experience creating comics and artwork. If you miss your lesson one month, don't worry! They don't expire! We'll set up a time that works best for you, even if it's after the month that you pledged.

If you have any questions about Patreon rewards, feel free to message me or leave a comment. Thank you all so much for your support! You help make this story of stories possible!
