You can clearly see in this document that the story changed quite a lot from its original script. And that's okay! As each page is completed, sometimes it takes a life of its own.

You'll see several sections in this one, including the original comic script, as well as a couple of other techniques I used to help flesh out the story, including the "Story Circle," which covers the major "story beats" of the chapter, as well as the "Short Story" version which I wrote before splitting it up into pages to, mostly as a brainstorming tool.
