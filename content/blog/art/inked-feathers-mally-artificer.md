I'll admit, it's been a while since I've played *Risk of Rain 2*, and the Artificer isn't even my main. But it's one of my favorites as far as visual design goes. And I do love to play it with friends! It's a little difficult to do solo, but playing together is a blast! (No pun intended.) I don't know if it's the kind of game Mally would enjoy, but she can also hover, and it was fun sticking a beak on that otherwise featureless orb of a face!
