Once again, a bit rushed, but I cleaned up the lines, added a background, and extended their bodies. A print of this will go with a Christmas tree that the group Utah Helping Paws has decorated and donated to the Salt Lake City Festival of Trees! The theme for our tree was, "A Furry Christmas," but we snuck a few scalies in too! ![:)](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/s/smile.gif) For those who don't know, people decorate and donate themed Christmas trees, and then they are sold or auctioned to individuals, businesses, or organizations, and all the proceeds go to a charity. Pretty fun!
