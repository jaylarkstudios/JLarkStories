Need a tissue, Ine? You've got a word balloon coming out of your nose! (Thankfully, I fixed that in the final page!)

This first chapter of the comic is one of several that is based on my own life experience. I served a religious mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for two years before attending university. When I returned home, my aunt and uncle who lived near the school let me stay in a room in their basement for my first two semesters for the very low price of occasionally mowing their lawn or shoveling their driveway... when I remembered to do so... 😅 It was a very nice arrangement of course, but as happened with Ine here, after two semesters they gently encouraged me to find a new place to live in order to have the full "Utah Valley college experience." In all seriousness, looking back years later, I am very grateful for the push. If they'd have let me, I probably would have stayed with them for another couple years or more. While it would have saved me rent money, I would have missed out on a great many valuable experiences, *some* of which went on to inspire more chapters of the comic!

Ine's Uncle Lane (the bearded dragon) and Aunt Jen (the puma) are loosely based on my own uncle and aunt I lived with. Those aren't their real names of course, but while they get comparatively little screen-time in the comic, I had them in mind while writing and drawing this chapter. If they're reading this now, thanks for giving me a home away from home as well as the nudge I needed to forge my own path. In so doing you became a very real catalyst for all the stories that followed, which I wouldn't trade for anything! ♥
