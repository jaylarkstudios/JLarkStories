My up-coming web comic has a name: Aww, Feathers!
This is just a simple banner for the site where I'll be hosting my comic. I may do a better one later down the road, but this will do for now.
The name "Aww, Feathers!" comes from one of the main characters, Ine (whose tail is featured on the banner). He uses the word "feathers" as sort of a replacement-swear, so it's kind of a catch-phrase of his.
Stay tuned for more updates!
