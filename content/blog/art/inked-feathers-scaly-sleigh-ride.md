There's a Christmas film that not many people know about that my family and I really enjoy called *[The Christmas Dragon](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3918686/)*. It's somewhat low budget, and it shows. But the creators did a good job with what they had, and the film has a lot of heart.

Anyway, we watched it the other night, and it gave me the random idea to think about what other dragons would do a good job pulling Santa's sleigh? A few candidates came to mind, but I loved the idea of Spyro, the Christmas Dragon! What he lacks in brawn, he makes up for in spunk and determination. So in addition to milk and cookies, be sure to leave a big haunch of roasted sheep tonight for Santa's newest helper! 😉🍖🐲
