June's turn to get the cartoon Inked Feathers treatment! You may be wondering why I'm going through this "cartoonification" process in the first place, and what I plan to do with these toony characters. The honest truth is I don't even know. I may not even use them after drawing them. As I've said numerous times already, it is a fun and I think instructive exercise. But while I think they're cute, I don't plan on changing the art style of *Aww, Feathers!* to match. I toyed with the idea very briefly, but no. It wouldn't feel right. It wouldn't feel like the style "matches" the story. I can see the use of simplifying and streamlining certain aspects of the comic's art style, but not to this extent. Still, perhaps there will be a mini story or something of that sort in which I could use them. We'll see.
