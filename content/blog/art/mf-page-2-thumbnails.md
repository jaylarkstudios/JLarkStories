Second page of thumbnails, where Mag casts the first spell of the story! The font I used for her spell is called Miskatonic, from the folks at Comic Craft. The words of the spell she uses to attempt to animate the little clay doll she made are something along the lines of, "Wood to bone, earth to flesh, air to breath, light to will." Naturally it could have said anything, since the font is not a real alphabet, but I thought it would be neat to give it some semblance of actual meaning.
