For today's exercise I drew from a photo reference of a Chinese water dragon, which, again, is one of the species in Ine's family tree. I cannot recommend highly enough the practice of drawing from photo references. You want to get better at drawing something specific? Find some photos of that thing, and start drawing them! First get good at drawing what you see, then you can begin to stylize and anthropomorphize!

I didn't finish filling in all the scales, but again, that's because the focus was on drawing from a reference, not necessarily on getting all the details. A future exercise will involve trying to get every tiny detail, so look forward to that one!
