More study on refining the structure of Ine's face. Something's not quite working the way I want it to. I have a way I like to draw Ine's face from the front, and from the side. The trouble is, they don't really match. If I were to take the front view, and then rotate it to the side view, the features wouldn't line up the way I currently draw them. It's okay though, I'll keep working on it. This is the first time I've ever seriously sat down to work this out, so it may take a while. And along the way, I've discovered some techniques to help me draw parts of the face that have been tricky for me in the past, so this has been a beneficial exercise either way.
