Focused on the lower body today: waist, legs, hips, tail, etc.

These daily drawing exercises have really been helping me to smooth out some of the areas where I've been a little rough. I highly recommend it! Once again, you can see the different exercises I've made for myself [here](https://www.patreon.com/posts/twenty-character-22767336). Yours don't have to be exactly the same as mine. The in fact, they don't even have to be about drawing. Whatever craft it is you want to improve on, choose at least twenty exercises that will help you improve, and then every day, do at least one of those. Don't let a day go by without doing one from that list! You can't help but get better at something you practice every day!
