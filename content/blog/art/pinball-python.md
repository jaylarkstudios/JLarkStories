*He's a pinball python,*
*I've never seen him fail,*
*That pinball python*
*Got such a nimble tail!*

My submission to [Goldquiver](https://goldquiver.deviantart.com)'s contest featuring his character, Quiv!  The goal was to draw him doing something snakes wouldn't ordinarily be able to do.  I got the idea from this picture featuring the same character:  [fav.me/d5tijob](http://fav.me/d5tijob)  The title is an obvious reference to *Pinball Wizard *by The Who, so I figured why not draw him actually playing pinball!  I didn't polish it up a whole lot, but I did add some color and shading and I like how it turned out!
