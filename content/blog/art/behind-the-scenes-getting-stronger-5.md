This page marks the first time in *Aww, Feathers!* that a character has ever been shown swearing. Scandalous, I know! Or maybe it doesn't count since it's depicted using grawlix? (Fun fact for those who may not know: "grawlix" is the term for those random symbols that are used to show that someone is cussing without actually showing the cuss. The more you $#&!@-ing know!)

I know it may seem silly to even be talking about this, but humor me! From when I first started *Aww, Feathers!* I've been determined to keep it family friendly. That goal is even subtly hinted at in the title of the whole comic! "Aww, Feathers!" is itself a replacement swear of sorts for the big bad f-bomb itself!

Side note, but have you ever noticed  how many replacements there are for the f-word? Freak, fetch, flip, fudge, frick, fekk, frikka-frakka... or is that just a Utah thing? But I digress...

I gotta admit, showing Michelle swearing, even in a way that's merely implied felt a bit "edgy" to me at first! But I think it's an effective way to communicate that she has a bit of gruffness to her personality, without (hopefully) offending those who don't want to see actual swear words in an ostensibly family-friendly comic. And besides, designing that word balloon was really quite fun! 😆
