A turn-around for some hair brushes I modeled for the student film at BYU which I'm participating in. (Sorry it takes a while to lead...) Yes, they do look pretty wacky; they're supposed to be that way! ![;)](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/w/wink.gif) They're just plain gray because I only did the models, not the texture and color, but I may add in a simple one later... we'll see.
The cool thing about them is another guy in the program helped me figure out a way to procedurally and automatically place all the little pins on the brushes to avoid having to do so manually. Pretty spiffy!
