This was a fun little experiment, based on a simple doodle I did last week! There's something very appealing to me in cartoon characters that have a lot of character and personality using very few lines and very simple shapes. That's not to say I like it better than more realistic or detailed characters and art styles. Both have their charms. And I think there's benefit in experimenting on both sides of that spectrum.

Not gonna lie... I'm tempted to spin off a gag strip using these characters, or ones like them. I'll have to add that to my ever growing pile of future project ideas, heh.
