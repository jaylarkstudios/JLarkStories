Hey all,

A while back I sent an *Aww, Feathers!* postcard to my family, and when they received it, it was smudged, perhaps from the mail sorting process. To test this, I sent one to myself from another address, just to see how it would fair in the mail. Sure enough, when I received it, it was also smudged (see the photo above). I'm going to be investigating different paper types to see if this problem can be fixed. In the meantime, if you have experienced similar problems with postcards you were sent, will you please take a picture of it and send it to me? I will gladly send you a replacement, in an envelope this time, and hopefully that will prevent the problem.

So sorry for any inconvenience this causes. But seriously, if you received a postcard in poor condition, don't hesitate to let me know. I'll get you a new one.
