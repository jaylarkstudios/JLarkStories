I have mixed feelings about Teen Titans Go! On the one hand, the few episodes I've seen have been disappointing in their content, and not nearly as entertaining as the original series (which actually had some semblance of a plot). On the other though, I really enjoy the art style. You may have noticed I really like the simple toony style. I haven't mastered it myself yet. Still, it's fun to try out once in a while.
