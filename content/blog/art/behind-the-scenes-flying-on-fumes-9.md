I've had an interest in game design for a long time. Even as a kid, I used to come up with ideas for new board games, card games, and video games, or modifications for existing ones. Little did I know how much the indie game scene would take off! Now anyone can take a shot at developing their own game and, if people find it interesting enough, get it funded and produced. I've toyed with the idea of bringing some of my own game ideas to life. But the fact always remains that to do so, I would have to take time away from my comic. For now, I've decided to not divide my focus any further than it currently is so I can keep telling this story. Paying homage to one of the game ideas I had was a fun way to give it a bit of life though. And who knows? Maybe someday when I have more time on my hands I'll be able to dedicate some effort to it.
