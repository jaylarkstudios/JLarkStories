Graphite stick and pencil.
This is the "Modified Contour Drawing" exercise from "Drawing on the Right Side of the Brain" by Betty Edwards.  I am way excited about how it turned out!  This is the first time anything I've drawn has turned out even close to being realistic looking!

Her book is awesome by the way; I highly recommend it, especially if you're like me and don't feel you can draw very well, but really want to learn!
