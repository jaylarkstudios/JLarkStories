I can't begin to tell you how quickly I would slam that subscribe button if Dil were to create a podcast!

To finish up this month's collection of Inked Feathers drawings, Dilawar the pancock gives a shoutout to to the entertainers who've kept on entertaining during the pandemic, even when there was nothing they felt less like doing. To the podcasters,  vloggers, artists, writers, composers, and animators, thank you for making us laugh and for making us cry. Thank you for keeping us informed about the world around us and for helping us escape from the world for a little while. Thank you for creating things that are new and unique and thank you for just being you and for putting a little bit of yourself into all that you do. Stay creative!

I hope you've enjoyed this month's collection of bonus drawings! If you would like to see more, [subscribe](http://join.awwfeathers.com) to unlock your exclusive access to the entire collection!
