Here's the very first page of thumbnail sketches for Mag & Fang! Keeping things nice and simple so it will be easy to change and rearrange things, and also so I can get a first draft done as quickly as possible in order to get feedback and progress to the revision stage.

Already I've made a couple changes from my original plan. I was going to give her a big ol' spellbook from which to read her incantations, but realized that I would then have to draw her carrying it around with her, or else come up with some way for her to stow it away. I figured it was simpler to just have her speaking the spells for herself. I also changed her "little friend" from a butterfly to a dragonfly. For some reason it just seems to fit better. But it may also simply be that it's easier to draw, lol!
