Man, I did NOT know what I was getting myself into when I started drawing all those dark scales! I'm glad I did it though. This turned out looking really cool! Which isn't hard of course, because snakes *are* cool! Especially Harley, the boa constrictor and scaly animal ambassador for Wild Wonders Utah! He's the subject of our final Wild Wonders themed Inked Feathers drawing. One final time, allow me to remind you that Harley, Juno, and all the other animals being cared for by the wonderful staff at Wild Wonders are in need of help. The pandemic has caused them to have to cancel a lot of their fundraising events this year, so anything you can do to help would be tremendous.

Check out [this post](https://www.patreon.com/posts/37960991) for idea on how you can help. Thanks to you all!
