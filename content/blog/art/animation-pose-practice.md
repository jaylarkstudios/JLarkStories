This is Norman, a free 3D model used for pose practice acquired from this site: [[link]](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?http://www.11secondclub.com/resources)
I did not make him, but I did use him in an animation assignment. We needed to put him in 10 different poses; 5 action and 5 emotion. He's basically like a very intricate puppet; you can change almost every aspect of his body position and expression. It was a lot of fun!
I haven't animated him yet; these are just a series of 10 different still images I made of him:
