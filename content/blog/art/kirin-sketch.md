Yet another creature from Neon's book; this time the Kirin (Chinese Unicorn).
I wanted to draw something that would force me to take my time, be patient, and pay attention to details. For obvious reasons, this guy fit the bill! My hand got a bit cramped after drawing in all those scales, haha!
I ran just short of room while drawing his horn... I think I need to start drawing on bigger paper.
Oh, and manes are hard.
