We had an awesome bishop in our singles' ward! For my non-mormon friends, in our church, young single adults (especially those away from home, like at school, etc.) are often organized into "Singles' Wards" for church and other activities. It's a blast! My wife and I were in the same ward before we got married. So I figured it would be fun to make an anthro version of our Bishop. So this is Bishop Ray, an anthro-dragon. A super fun-loving guy who loves the members of his ward, and is tickled to death to watch them fall in love with each other... as was the case for Ine and June!
