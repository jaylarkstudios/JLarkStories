I've had more than one friend cock an eyebrow and/or give me that look like I just said something dirty when they learn about my interest in the furry fandom. This is the easiest way I've found to set the record straight. Usually after explaining it this way they nod their heads and go, "Oh..." Communication. It works. ![:-)](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/s/smile.gif)
I know the diagram may not be "to scale" and may oversimplify the distinctions a little bit, but for me, it works. If you think this describes you as well, feel free to share it with those who don't quite understand.
Quick note: Some people automatically associate the fandom with spiritualism, shamanism, etc. I know some people have legitimate religious beliefs that are similar, and I'm not putting them down; this is simply my way of explaining that I'm in the furry fandom simply for good, clean fun. Again, I know this diagram doesn't describe everyone, but it does describe me.
