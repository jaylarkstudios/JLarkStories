(Sorry it's not any bigger... it's the only way I could fit the whole thing on the page, and the download makes it way too huge! You should hopefully be able to read everything at this size.)
By far my favorite project in my Intro to Art class! We were told to make a book cover for a children's book. I did my best to make it really look like a book cover; I found a free UPC generator ([[link]](https://www.deviantart.com/users/outgoing?http://www.barcoding.com/upc/)) and just used my birthdate, lol! Then I took it to my school's printing place and used their ginormous poster printer to make a beautiful hard copy, which I then laminated and left overnight wrapped around another book underneath a huge pile of books (to make it conform to the proper shape).
I had a lot of people compliment me on it; they said it actually looks like something you'd see on a bookstore shelf! I absolutely love the way this turned out! ...And it actually kinda makes me want to write the actual book, lol!
