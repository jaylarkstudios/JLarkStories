Yes! Got it! On a previous sketchbook post, I pointed out that I didn't care for the more dog-like face I gave Chris in his side view. I think I've refined it here though to the point that I really do like it! And again, this pleases me greatly because I want to give my characters more animal-like traits. I like the way the nose is looking as well. I must credit the book I'm currently reading, *The Cartoonist's Big Book of Drawing Animals* by Chris Hart. I borrowed a couple techniques from the book which helped me arrive at the style above. I'm really excited by this you guys! I love that I'm being more deliberate with my characters and really drilling down to the details! It's yielding great rewards for me!
