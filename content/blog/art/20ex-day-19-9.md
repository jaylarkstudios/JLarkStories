C-c-c-c-combo breaker!

Until now, I've been doing my character drawing exercises in order. Today however, I decided to go back and do more action poses. It's an excellent warm up before sitting down to draw a finished piece! As you can see, I let myself stay loose and messy. I'd encourage the same thing when you're practicing your own gesture drawing. Focus on getting capturing the movement and energy more than precise details.
