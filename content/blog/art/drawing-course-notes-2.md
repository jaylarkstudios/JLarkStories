Today we focused on *line*. Did a few blind contour drawings. I remember doing a similar exercise before. You look at an object and try to draw its outline without looking at the paper while you draw it. The point isn't to get it as accurate as possible (thank goodness!) but rather to train you to pay attention to the lines that actually make up the object, rather than paying attention to the drawing you're creating. They say you should spend most of your time looking at the subject you're drawing, not the drawing itself.
