A new character!  Dil is the second character from "Aww, Feathers!" to get a character sheet.  Dil's full name is Dilawar, which is an Indian name (from India, not Native American) meaning "Courageous."
"Wait, what's this?" you say, "Anthros and ferrals in the same universe?!  Doesn't that violate some law of... physics or something?"  Don't be alarmed!  In the world of AF, there are both anthro (2-legged) and ferral (4-legged) characters.  That's all I'll reveal for now.  Stay tuned to learn more!

I'm very happy with Dil!  He was very fun to create.  A challenge, certainly, but well worth it!  I've got at least a couple more character sheets in the works showcasing more characters for my upcoming webcomic, so keep an eye out!
