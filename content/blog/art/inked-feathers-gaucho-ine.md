Fire up the asado and pass the mate (MAH-tay)! Ine served a religious mission in Argentina for two years, so he feels like he's got a bit of gaucho in him to balance out the gringo.

This is my first experiment with midtone paper *and* a white pen for adding highlights, and I love the way it looks! I plan on making more drawings using this style throughout the month of September, so if you'd like your exclusive access to the rest, make sure you're subscribed to Aww, Feathers! on Patreon at the [Inked Feathers: Bronze](http://patreon.com/join/awwfeathers) level or higher!
