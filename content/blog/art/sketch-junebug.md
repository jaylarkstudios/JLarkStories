Attract these adorable but elusive critters to your garden by hiding sardines among your rose bushes. They get along great with hummingbirds and are easily identified by their shiny lavender colored carapace and high pitched chattering.

This is the first member-exclusive Sketchbook Club entry. Thanks so much to those who've pledged a little extra! I've had this idea for a little while and it's fun to finally give her form! Later this month she'll be sent to one of our Silver (or higher) level members.
