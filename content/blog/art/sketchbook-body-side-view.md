I remember when I first figured out the trick of drawing figures with curved backs instead of perfectly straight and being amazed at how much that alone made them appear more lifelike and less stiff! Look at yourself in the mirror from the side and stand naturally. You'll see that your back is NOT straight. Your rib cage tilts one way and your hips tilt the opposite way.
