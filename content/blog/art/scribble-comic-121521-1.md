Ever since the panini, I've been getting a lot of really wimpy colds. You know the kind: just enough to make your day kind of a bummer, but not enough to justify taking the day off (provided you can work remotely). I've tested negative for covid every time, so they really are just common colds, which is weird because I normally don't get sick very often. Could be stress I suppose. If there's a silver lining, it's that it's made me pay closer attention to my physical health and how I take care of my body.
