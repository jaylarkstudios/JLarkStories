God is an artist, and he paints with many colors. 🎨 We are better together.

I've been thinking for a while about what to say and how to say it. I knew I wanted to say *something*, so I finally created this. I hope it adequately conveys my feelings. I truly do love and value members of all races, nationalities, orientations, etc. Our differences make us and the world we live in beautiful!

The new characters shown here are, in order of appearance, a crocodile skink, black lab, red-winged blackbird, and orca. There is no direct relation between specific species in *Aww, Feathers!* and human races in real life. However, as hinted in a few earlier comic pages, there are sometimes tensions between the different species. Moreover, I chose species with darker skin, fur, or feathers in an effort to show solidarity with those who've endured violence, injustice, and discrimination because of the color of their own skin. At the same time, showing species pairs that are very similar to one another in spite of visual differences is meant to demonstrate that in many important ways we are also the same. The word "species" is used colloquially in *Aww, Feathers!* to refer to anthros whose appearances matches those of different animals. However, in reality, they all belong to the same species. Likewise, we all belong to the same human family. The same human race.

So of course everyone matters. And *that is precisely why* when one group is disproportionately affected by violence, poverty, and intolerance, the rest of us should stop what we're doing long enough to take notice, speak up, and do what we can to help. Because *black lives matter.*

Thank you for taking the time to read. Take care, be well, and remember, you matter. 💙
