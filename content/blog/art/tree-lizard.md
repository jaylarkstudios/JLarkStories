All finished!
We were camping at our family's favorite camp site that has these awesome trees... I think they're willows. Anyhoo, they're way fun to climb. I had my sister climb one and pose for me, and this was the result! ^^ I tried out some new coloring techniques that were kind of fun and turned out looking pretty good. This is one of my better attempts at a 3/4 view of the head as well, so I'm very happy with it!
Much thanks to [![kyo-katt](https://a.deviantart.net/avatars/k/y/kyo-katt.gif?1)](https://kyo-katt.deviantart.com/) for the pose, and to [![eenafarore](https://a.deviantart.net/avatars/e/e/eenafarore.png?9)](https://eenafarore.deviantart.com/), [![kevindragon](https://a.deviantart.net/avatars/k/e/kevindragon.jpg?9)](https://kevindragon.deviantart.com/), and [![firequill](https://a.deviantart.net/avatars/f/i/firequill.gif?10)](https://firequill.deviantart.com/) for help with drawing the tail from behind; I was struggling with how to do that for a while there.
Once again, any comments or suggestions on the shading would be awesome!
