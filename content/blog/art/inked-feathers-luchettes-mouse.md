I just celebrated my 8th Featherversary! To start off the day, I asked followers on social media to supply me with some random words. They could be foods, activities, adjectives, etc. I took the suggestions, made a list, and then randomly picked a character from the comic and a random word from that list as the subject of an *Aww, Feathers! *themed sketch!

The first Festherversary sketch of the day paired Luchette with the word "mouse". This model is specially designed for hooved species. I also made her ears and face more gazelle-like than I've drawn her in the past, and I really like how it turned out! I plan to incorporate these updates into her design in future comic pages.
