2020 was terrible.

But we have reasons to hope for a much better year in 2021. Soon we may even be able to return to everyday activities like taking a shower, leaving the house, putting on pants, watching a movie on a screen larger than your laptop, and eating out without constantly forgetting you're wearing a mask!

To celebrate the upcoming new year, and to give you a place to jot down all your plans, I've put together the first ever Aww, Feathers! themed calendar, with thirteen illustrations as unique and colorful as the characters featured in them! It's marked with major U.S. and international holidays, as well as a few fun ones sprinkled throughout, giving you something to commemorate or celebrate every month of the promising new year!
