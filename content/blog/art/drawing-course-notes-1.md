I'm excited to be taking an online drawing course! I have very little formal art training, and while I've definitely seen improvement just by the amount of practice I've done on my own, I'd like to refine my technique and learn some new skills. I decided it'd be fun to share my notes and sketches from the course I'm taking. 😃

Dang, that eye actually looks pretty good. To be fair, I was following along with the instructor, doing pretty much everything he did. Still, it's nice to see I can get something pretty good looking if I put enough time into it. Besides, the lizard eye next to it I did on my own (with a photo reference). 👁️
