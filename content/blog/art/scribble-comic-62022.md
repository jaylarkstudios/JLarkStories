As a family we just started watching the Land Before Time tv series, because yes that is a thing, and minutes into the first episode I already have so many questions!! 😲

Granted, this was made before streaming gave people the luxury of watching whatever series they wanted from the very beginning, so writers couldn't guarantee which episodes a viewer would see. To get around this, instead of putting all their exposition in the first episode, they would gradually drip it out over the course of several episodes. It does work, it's just not what we're used to!
