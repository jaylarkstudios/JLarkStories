I got a new comic lettering font not too long ago, which turned out to be useful for this page in particular. It's one that Blambastic (by Blambot) looks just a tad off-kilter, which happens to work great for when a character is yelling or otherwise stressed. In addition, it has two slightly different versions of each glyph, which is handy for words or exclamations that have the same letter twice in a row, or for using multiple of the same punctuation mark. Look closely at June's exclamation in the second panel, and you'll see what I mean. It looks better than if every N, H, and exclamation point looked exactly the same. Lettering is an art all on its own, and I'm still very much a novice! But whenever I learn a simple trick like this one, I try to be on the lookout for opportunities to use them!
