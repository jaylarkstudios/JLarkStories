I'm not quite familiar enough with Mickey Mouse to trying drawing him quickly while following along with the instructor, haha! Still, it was a good insight about breaking characters down into their basic shapes. I didn't know Disney deliberately used very simple shapes (circles and rectangles mostly) when designing Mickey in order to make him easy to copy for the animators who'd be working with him.
