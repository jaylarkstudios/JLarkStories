The last of the three artist trading cards I made for this swap, this one with a bit of a different tone. One life is ending, but in return it sustains other lives. Does life ever really end, or is it like energy, an indestructible force that just moves from one place or state to another?
I enjoyed doing these! It's neat to see what you can do with just a 2.5"x3.5" card. I hope to participate in more card swaps in the future!
