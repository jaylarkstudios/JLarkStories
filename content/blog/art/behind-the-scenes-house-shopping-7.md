This apartment very closely resembles my own apartment; it was just easy to remember that layout and map it on to this one where Ine moves.

I'm kind of embarrassed that I made it look so empty though. No chairs or tables or anything, despite the fact that there were still three other roommates living here. But that's just another lesson learned. I now try to keep some ready-made background items on hand that I can use to make a space feel more "lived in" when needed.
