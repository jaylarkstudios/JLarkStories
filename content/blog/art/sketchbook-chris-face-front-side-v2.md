I got some good feedback on my [previous drawing](https://www.patreon.com/posts/sketchbook-chris-24200220) of Chris's profile and front view and drew an updated version accordingly. I made the relative size between his jowls and chin more consistent across the two views, fixed the shape of his ears a little bit, gave his brow ridge a more gentle slope, paid closer attention to the difference in eye shape between the two views, and finally made his nose look a bit more 3D.

I'll seek feedback on this newer version as well, but I think this is pretty darn close to my final design for Chris. I plan on making a full-fledged character sheet for each of my main characters, including a full body view from the front and side, several key expressions, closeups of patterns and markings, etc. So expect more in-depth character studies and refinements in the future!
