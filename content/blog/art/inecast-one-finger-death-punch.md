Last night I tried kind of a fun experiment. I streamed a game for a little while, and then drew a mini comic about my impressions of the game. Last night's game was One Finger Death Punch, a game about fighting stick figures using only the left and right mouse buttons! The game does an excellent job at making the player feel powerful and downright kick-awesome! The mechanics are easy to learn, but it's possible to put some effort into really mastering the timing in order to maximize your score. Good for a quick, casual game, or an afternoon of replaying the same level over and over, gradually memorizing the sequence of enemies and working your way to a higher score.

It was fun! I plan to do it again in the near future with a different game. If you want to watch while I'm doing a game and/or art stream, check me out on twitch at twitch.tv/awwfeathers
