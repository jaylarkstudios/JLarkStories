Just a collage of a bunch of stuff from my sketch book after--for the umpteenth time--setting the goal to draw at least one thing every day. This time I'm doing a lot better at sticking with that goal, and I'm seeing good results. ![:)](https://e.deviantart.net/emoticons/s/smile.gif)
They're mostly reptiles, with an avian and a couple of wolves thrown in there. I'm especially pleased that I'm getting better at showing different expressions, although as you can see, most of them are from the side view. My front view skills are so-so, and my 3/4 needs more work, but I'm getting better.
