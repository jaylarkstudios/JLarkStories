Gender: Male

Species: Blue-banded pancock, quadruped. Dil's biological father was a peacock and his mother was a panther.

Personality: Good-natured but a bit mischieveous; enjoys playing pranks on his friends, but never with the intent of hurting anyone's feelings. Usually easy-going and occasionally a bit careless. Generally tolerant of others and not easily offended. He's a good student, but doesn't particularly enjoy school. He likes outdoor summer activities, but avoids going out in the winter months as much as possible.

History: Dil was born in India but orphaned at a young age. He was taken in by a school for underprivileged children. An LDS otter couple was volunteering there at the time and took interest in him and eventually adopted him and took him to their home in the U.S. Dil was able to get a high school education and even earn a scholarship to attend university, though he's still unsure what to major in.Dil is another character of my webcomic, Aww, Feathers! He's Ine's fun-loving roommate, but he's more than just comic relief; he has a tender side to him once you get to know him. Dil is a quadruped. In the world of Aww, Feathers!, quadrupeds (colloquially referred to as "ferals") are just like their bipedal counterparts; they just have body proportions that more closely match actual animals. But quadrupeds can be born to bipedal parents, and visa versa.
