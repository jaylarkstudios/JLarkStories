Getting back into the habit of doing some daily sketches, for warmup or practice or just to try out something new. I've found I really enjoy sketching with ballpoint pen on graphing paper. Something about the cheapness of the materials just really reduces the pressure to draw something "good," and lets me really just cut loose and draw!
