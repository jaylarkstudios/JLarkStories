"Now, I need you to reach into this hat and pull out the first card your hand touches, *but* you must do so while yodeling. Louder... The cards can't hear you! There you go! Now reach in there and pick a card, but whatever you do *don't stop yodeling!* Perfect! Now kindly show the card to the audience. Was this the card you picked from the deck? No...? Ah well. The trick may have flopped, but at least it was entertaining! ...You can stop yodeling now."
