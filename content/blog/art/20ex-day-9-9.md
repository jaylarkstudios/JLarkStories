Sunday's exercise: gesture drawing... in which I frequently forgot to add  the tail because none of the models I was using had them. 😂 Lack of tails notwithstanding, I do want to give a shoutout to a super useful tool for practicing gesture/figure drawing: [https://line-of-action.com/](https://line-of-action.com/) It has a large collection of action poses, anatomy studies, facial expressions, and even animal photos to practice from.
