Y'all have heard of Movember, right? I tried it once, and for the life of me I couldn't grow more than a gross, scraggly, patchwork of hair on my upper lip that looked like I'd only been working on it for maybe a couple days, not a whole month. Poking fun at Ine's similar inability to grow "facial feathers" was a fun little self-insert! Though I'm glad I get to just shave instead of having to pluck every individual hair! 😬
