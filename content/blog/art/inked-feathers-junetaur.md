Being a taur means always having a comfy place to sit and read!

Now, I know what you're thinking: that's not what a dolphin looks like! But of course, if I'd made June all dolphin below, she'd have looked like a mermaid, and we already had a mer-month! So I experimented with a different quadruped for her lower half, and I think it turned out really cute!
