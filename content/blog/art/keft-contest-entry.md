Name: Keft
Gender: Male
Species: Mountain Dragon
Age: 73 (young adult for his species)
Personality:
Cautious and mistrusting towards strangers, except for children. He enjoys the company of children and is very protective of them.
History:
As a child, Keft wanted what nearly all dragonlings his age wanted: to one day become a mighty hunter or fighter, respected and feared. He was born a runt however, and even as he began to grow, developed no extraordinary skills or strength. Then one day he got into a tussle with another young dragon who clawed him in the face, blinding him in one eye, ending his hopes for becoming even a modest hunter forever.
At first Keft was bitter and for a long time felt like he was useless and a burden to his tribe. But with time he was given a job to watch over the younglings while the adults went hunting. He quickly discovered that he liked watching over the children, entertaining and comforting them.
