My exercise on Saturday was to do a gender-bend or species swap between two characters, but I altered it slightly. I took Ine and Dil, who are normally biped and quadruped respectively, and put Ine on all fours and stood Dil upright. I added a few details to make Ine look a bit more feral as well. Had a bit of trouble figuring out Ine's toes, but otherwise I think it turned out kind of neat!
