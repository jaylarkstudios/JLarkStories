I'm excited to announce a new, special gift just for *Aww, Feathers!* Patreon subscribers! From now until the end of the month, **all new and existing** patrons at the $5 level or higher will get a digital copy of the "Aww, Feathers! Patron Sketchbook" as a DRM-free EPUB and PDF!

This gift will **only** be available to Patreon subscribers at the $5 level or higher, and **only** through the end of this month!

**What's inside?**
This eBook contains over a hundred *Aww, Feathers!* themed drawings and sketches, including the entire 2018 Inked Feathers collection, the first few months of the 2019 collection, and dozens of sketches done at conventions and other events! If you're unfamiliar with the Inked Feathers drawing collection, here are a few [public previews](https://www.patreon.com/awwfeathers/posts?tag=InkedFeathersPreview) to give you an idea of what to expect! All the rest are patron-exclusives!

**What if I'm not a patron yet?**
Then now's your opportunity to not only gain access to exclusive patron-only perks and bonus *Aww, Feathers!* material, but to also take advantage of this special offer! Click [here](https://www.patreon.com/join/awwfeathers) and choose the $5 level or higher before the end of May to be eligible to receive this special gift!

**What if I'm already a $5+ subscriber?**
Then you're good to go! There's nothing else you need to do other than keep your subscription current through the end of the month!

**What if I'm currently a $1 or $2 subscriber?**
In order to be eligible for this special offer, you'll need to switch to the $5 tier or higher. Click [here](https://www.patreon.com/join/awwfeathers) to change your subscription level (you will be charged the difference between whatever you've already pledged this month and the amount of your newly chosen tier). Once you do, you will immediately gain access to additional perks, including the exclusive *Inked Feathers* drawing collection, and you'll also receive this special offer after the end of the month!

**What do I need to do once I subscribe?**
Nothing! As long as you are a $5+ subscriber by the end of the month, you will qualify and I will send you a link to download your  ebook!

[Click here now to join and get your special gift!](https://www.patreon.com/join/awwfeathers) 

If there are any other questions, let me know! Have a great day!
