That last line by Ine's dad is *totally* something my own dad would have said! In fact, I think he might have said it at some point to gently cajole me into initiating my own housing search.

Looking at the completed page now, I really think I was being too clever by half with the colors and patterns in the background, filling in the spaces between panels. I was trying to evoke the feeling of frustration, stress, and worry, but I think it just comes across as busy and kinda weird. But it's another thing I learned and refined as I went along.
