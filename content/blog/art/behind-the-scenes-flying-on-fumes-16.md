I got to the end of the process of creating this page and realized I hadn't been consistent with the placement of the lunch bag in Chris' hand... In one panel on the previous page it's there, then it's not. Then in the first panel of this page it's not there, then it is. Guh... 😖

It's not a huge deal, and it's plausible he could have set it down and then picked it back up again. Plus, there's a decent chance that no one will notice. At times like this, a comic artist needs to decide whether going back and fixing something will be worth the extra time. Once again, any time spent fixing this mistake is time that could be spent on the next page.

My plan is to see if anyone notices it (without me pointing it out to them 😉) and decide what to do from there. I may just leave it be. If a bunch of readers find it distracting though, then I may go back and fix it. I guess we'll see!
