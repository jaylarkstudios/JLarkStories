Here he is!  I've been meaning to make a character sheet for Ine for a long time now and finally did it!  This is Ine's character sheet for the *Aww, Feathers!* comic I'm making, which begins at the end of Ine's first semester at college.
As I mentioned before, many of the events in AF will be loosely based on events from my own life, and Ine in many ways represents myself.
I like this one quite a lot!  I think it's only my second attempt at a completely polished full-body pic of Ine.  The anatomy is a bit off in a few places, and I'm still practicing my shading, but I'm pretty happy with the face especially.

Fun fact:  The stuff you see in the background came from actual notes I took while in college!
