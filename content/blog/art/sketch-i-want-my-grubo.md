A while back One of my favorite webcomic creators Dave Kellett challenged other artists to draw something in the simple style of those old UPA animations. You might not know what I'm talking about, and that's okay. I only know about it because I took a History of Animation class in college. This sketch is based on my favorite animation in the aforementioned category!
