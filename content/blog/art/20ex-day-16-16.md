Moving right along! This exercise was to draw a completely new, randomly generated character. The words you see at the top were the ones I was given, and I interpreted them as the drawing you see here! I think it's a useful exercise, to encourage you to keep trying new things instead of only drawing the characters you're already used to drawing.

Who knows who she's talking to on that headset... Maybe it's whoever she's baking that bread for.
