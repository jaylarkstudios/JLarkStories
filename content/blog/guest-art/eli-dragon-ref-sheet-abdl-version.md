Created: Aug 21, 2020

Name: Eli

Gender: Male, Demiboy

Age: Young adult

Species: Winged Eastern Dragon

Likes: Caring friends, hugging soft things, hand-crafted diet sodas, board games

Dislikes: Being teased, sitting still, scary movies, homework

Personality: Eli is very anxious, shy, and emotional. He frequently chews on things when he's nervous (which is often). When nothing else is around, he'll even bite his knuckles or just grind his teeth. Eventually he got a mouth-guard which can stand up to his chewing and help protect his teeth at the same time. He also found he enjoys wearing diapers. He doesn't use them for waste, but rather for comfort. In addition, he enjoys using a pacifier, preferably custom made with a sturdy nib that he can safely gnaw on. While he doesn't use baby items in public, he sometimes does when alone or with close friends. They make him feel safe and accepted, because people naturally want to protect and care for a baby, not tease them for crying. That's all Eli wants from his friends, and he tries to make them feel loved and cared for in turn.

Eli mostly identifies as male, but enjoys some things typically considered "girly" by others, like flowers, hearts, dolls, and frilly pink diapers, skirts, and other outfits.
