The world's longest running daily webcomic! Takes place in a world where animals are intelligent and have formed civil society, but sometimes give in to their more primal urges. Frequently uses animal traits as a metaphor for current political and social issues, though there are also plenty of strips that aren't political at all. There's a fair amount of implied death, as the characters sometimes hunt one another, and it's sometimes off-putting how casually they do so for what is otherwise a light-hearted comic.
