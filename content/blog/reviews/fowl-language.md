A comic about a a family of ducks, drawn in a loose cartoony style and full of relatable observations on the struggles of parenting and adult life. Fair warning, this comic is very deserving of its name. There is frequent use of profanity and sarcasm. Still, it is also funny and sometimes even heartwarming.
