A rat practicing medicine and living in Australia. The story takes place in a society of animals that are intelligent and civilized, but that still prey on each other. The strip contains a mix of animal and medical related jokes and visual gags, as well as the more serious story of those who are trying to put an end to the practice of predation. A handful of comics featuring furry or animal characters address the question of what the meat-eating species do when they need to eat; Doc Rat is my favorite treatment of that question.
