Here are some of the webcomics (in alphabetical order) that I enjoy and recommend to others. Each has a brief description, as well as an informal rating; I'm not a ratings board of course, but I do offer my opinion for which ones are family-friendly and which ones are better suited for a more mature audience:

- **K (Kids):** Appropriate for all ages, but especially geared towards children. Some older readers may find it infantile.
- **G (General):** Family-friendly; could be enjoyed by just about anyone. Very little, if any, objectionable content.
- **PG (Parental Guidance):** Mostly okay for kids, but parents may want to take a look first. A few minor things to look out for.
- **T (Teen):** Intended for older readers; small children probably shouldn't read.
- **M (Mature):** Definitely not for children; still SFW, but with content or topics that could be upsetting or inappropriate for some readers.

I'm still working on this list. (I follow a LOT of comics!) But if you know of any other comics you think belong on this list, especially those that are furry-related, clean, and with a decent archive, let me know!
