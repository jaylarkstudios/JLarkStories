"The story is serious, the characters are hilarious" (Jeff Kapalka). Drive takes place in a future in which humans steal faster than light travel and begin colonizing the galaxy. Unfortunately, the original creators of the tech want it back. Featuring a varied collection of extraterrestrial races, each with distinctive look, culture, and abilities.
