Furry characters attending school in Utah Valley. Lots of humor, a touch of drama, and plenty of commentary and good-natured satire on Utah Latter-day Saint culture. It should come as no surprise that this is the webcomic that inspired me to create *Aww, Feathers!* I started reading *Furry Experience* soon after joining the furry fandom myself. It made me want to create my own comic in a very similar setting, and the creator, Ellen Natalie, was very supportive and encouraging!

The characters are fun and relatable. While the world they live in is occasionally exaggerated for comedic effect, the lived experiences feel very real. Every character feels unique, with their own believable backgrounds, hopes, heartaches, and quirks.
