A collage from a bunch of photos my girlfriend took of me this past weekend! Ine was made using green tye-dye fleece and shaggy red mongolian fur. The blue t-shirt is from the Provo Monster Mile and has the logo I designed! The other photos are from a halloween party.
Ine was a pretty big hit! Lots of people were impressed with the costume. Little kids were especially like, "Whoa!" It took them a while to warm up to Ine, but once they saw that he was friendly, they loved him! <3 I got called lizard, dinosaur, alligator, dragon, and even Barney, lol!
Ah, what a blast!
